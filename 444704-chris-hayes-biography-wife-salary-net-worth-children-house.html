Chris Hayes' biography: All In, wife, MSNBC, net worth, and podcast

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 15, 2024

Chris Hayes is an American political commentator, television news anchor, activist, editor, and author. Millions watch his program on MSNBC because he is lively and knowledgeable. Growing up, Hayes only wanted to be an actor but found himself writing in the print media and later transitioned to hosting shows on television. Chris became the youngest anchor on MSNBC at age 34 and has received many awards, including multiple Emmys, over the 20 years he has worked in the media.

Chris Hayes presents news on MSBNC on weeknights at 8:00 pm ET. His program, All In with Chris Hayes, covers everything from American trade to politics and current affairs. Furthermore, Chris is the author of Twilight of the Elites and an editor-at-large at The Nation.

Who is Chris Hayes?

Christopher Loffredo Hayes is an American Emmy award-winning news anchor, political commentator, author, podcast host, activist, and editor. He hosts the All In with Chris Hayes show on MSBNC on weeknights.

Where is Chris Hayes from?

The American news anchor was born in Norwood, a working-class residential neighborhood in the northwest Bronx of New York City, USA.

How old is Christopher Hayes?

The MSBNC political commentator is 44 years old as of 2023. He was born on 28 February 1979.

What is Chris Hayes' ethnic background?

He hails from a white ethnic background and has Irish-Italian ancestry. Chris Hayes' father is an Irish Catholic, while his mum is Italian.

Who are Chris Hayes' parents?

Chris Hayes' father, Roger Hayes, spent several years in community organizing in New York, while his mother, Geri Hayes, was a school teacher. Roger now works as an assistant commissioner for the NYC Department of Health, while Geri has a job in the NYC Department of Education.

Does Chris Hayes have siblings?

He has a brother named Luke Hayes and a sister called Lynn Hayes.

Who has Chris Hayes' brother worked for?

His brother, Luke, served as the campaign manager for US Congressman Jamaal Bowman in 2020 and worked on Barack Obama's 2008 and 2012 presidential campaigns.

Where did Chris Hayes study?

Hayes attended Hunter College High School in Carnegie Hill, Manhattan's Upper East Side region. In 1997, he moved to Rhode Island to study philosophy of mathematics at Brown University.

Was Chris Hayes an actor?

At the university, he wrote and acted in stage plays. Hayes graduated in 2001 and moved to Chicago to pursue his dream in theatre but ended up working in the mainstream media.

Career history

Chris Hayes started his journalism career in August 2001 as a contributor for Chicago Border (an independent weekly political newspaper).

In 2003, he began a four-year stint at In These Times (a monthly political news magazine) as a senior editor. Between 2005 and 2006, Chris was a Schumann Center Writing Fellow for the same magazine.

After that, he wrote for The Nation from 2006 to 2007 and was made the publication's Washington, DC, editor in 2007. Other publications Hayes has written for include Time, The Guardian, New York Times Magazine, and Chicago Reader.

Between 2008 and 2010, Chris was an adjunct English professor at St. Augustine College, Chicago, and a Bernard L. Schwartz fellow at the New America Foundation.

The journalist joined television in 2010 as a guest host for The Rachel Maddow Show on MSNB and later landed more hosting gigs on shows like The Ed Show, Countdown With Keith Olbermann, and The Last Word With Lawrence O’Donnell.

In 2011, MSNBC gave Chris a two-hour weekend morning show, Up with Chris Hayes. The program allowed him to delve deep into America's political, business, and economic matters.

Hayes began hosting the All In With Chris Hayes on MSNBC in 2013 at age 34, thus the youngest host of a prime-time show in America. Chris Hayes' show won an Emmy in 2015 and 2018.

What is Chris Hayes' podcast?

Chris launched his weekly podcast, Why Is This Happening, in 2018. The show features interviews with politicians, journalists, activists, academics, and writers. Besides this, he shares the biggest news and political stories on All In With Chris Hayes Apple podcasts.

Chris Hayes' books

The American political commentator has published several books. His first book, Twilight of the Elites: America After Meritocracy, was released in June 2012, and the second one, A Colony in a Nation, came out in 2017.

Who is Chris Hayes’ wife?

Chris Hayes's partner, Kate A. Shaw, is a lawyer and a professor. Furthermore, she is the daughter of veteran Chicago journalist Andy Shaw, who once worked for ABC News.

How did Chris Hayes meet his wife?

Kate and Hayes were both undergraduates at Brown University when they in 2001. At the time, she was pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in religious and gender studies. The two married in 2007, almost a year after she graduated from the Northwestern Pritzker School of Law in 2006.
