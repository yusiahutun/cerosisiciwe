Short Description of Donnie Yen

Early Life

Yen was born on 27 July 1963 in Guangzhou, Guangdong of China and now he is 52 years old He is the son of newspaper editor Klyster Yen and martial arts master Bow Sim Mark. At the age of 4 Yen began practicing martial arts from his mother, who taught him wushu. And tai chi until the age of 11 when his family migrated to Boston, MA. Similarly, he continued mastering wushi and tai chi.

After getting a huge interest in martial arts he finally began getting into several different martial art styles, including taekwondo, kickboxing, boxing, karate. After passing sixteen his parents sent him to Beijing Wushu Academy to train Chinese martial arts under Master Wu Bin, for three years.
