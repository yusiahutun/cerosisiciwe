Halsey turns heads in a sheer black dress at the Lanvin showduring Paris Fashion Week

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Halsey showcased her incredible figure in a sheer black dress at the Lanvin show during Paris Fashion Week on Sunday. 

The singer, 28, turned heads as she made a grand arrival to watch the Fall/Winter 2023 fashion show.  

She looked nothing short of sensational as she flashed her dark underwear through the eye catching sleeveless woven number. 

The hitmaker added inches to her frame in a pair of towering coordinated heels and accessorised with dazzling turquoise earrings. 

Halsey, who's real name is Ashley Frangipane, applied a dramatic palette of makeup including a bold glittering blue eye shadow and bronzed cheeks. 

Dressed to impress: Halsey showcased her incredible figure in a sheer black dress at the Lanvin show during Paris Fashion Week on Sunday

Bold: The singer, 28, flashed her dark underwear through the eye catching sleeveless woven number which she teamed with coordinated towering heels 

The songwriter styled her long dark tresses poker straight as they cascaded down her back from a centre parting. 

Halsey completed her ensemble by matching a chunky turquoise ring and her manicure to her eye makeup.  

She sat front row at the star-studded fashion show with singer Avril Lavigne, 38, and model Josephine Skriver, 29. 

Avril cut a smart figure in black tailored suit which she wore with bold black eye makeup while Josephine looked effortlessly chic in a yellow blazer dress. 

The beauty, who welcomed her first child, Ender, back in 2021, made her runway debut amid her time at PFW by walking for the brand, Pressiat, on Tuesday. 

Halsey donned a semi-sheer, corseted bodysuit that contained a blue and black cheetah print and wore a matching skirt that fell down straight towards her ankles.  

On Instagram, Halsey expressed her excitement to have had the opportunity to strut on the catwalk, and penned to her 31 million followers, 'I walked PFW for the first time today with @pressiat_ and it was terrifying and amazing!!!!' 

'Thank you Vincent for having me, and congratulations to you and team on such a stunning collection and iconic show!' the star further added, referencing to the creative director of the brand, Vincent Garnier Pressiat.

Amazing: Halsey, who's real name is Ashley Frangipane, applied a dramatic palette of makeup including a bold glittering blue eye shadow which she teamed with matching dazzling earrings

Eye catching: The songwriter styled her long dark tresses poker straight as they cascaded down her back from a centre parting

Details: Halsey rocked a chunky bold turquoise ring and a matching manicure as she headed inside for the fashion show

While at the Givenchy show, she also opened up to WWD about the experience, and admitted to the outlet, 'It was really scary.' 

'There’s nothing to distract you. When you’re performing, you’re focusing on the words and the songs and doing a great job,' she continued. 'When I was walking, all I could think was ‘Don’t trip, don’t trip.’ It was like I forgot how to walk on the two feet God gave me.' 

The mother of one also revealed that the time on the runway flew by. 'You spend the whole day getting ready for 30 seconds. But you know, the pictures are forever, so it was really exciting.'

They also gushed about the creative director of Givenchy, Matthew M. Williams, and stated, 'I've been a huge fan of his forever, through all his brands, all his projects and the work that he’s done with Lady Gaga is incredible. I’ve been following his journey for a long time.' 

The singer is known for her personal sense of style and bold looks, and is no stranger when it comes to makeup, having launched her own Vegan beauty brand, About Face, in 2021. 

She sat front row at the star-studded fashion show with singer Avril Lavigne, 38, (middle) and model Josephine Skriver, 29 (right)

Avril cut a smart figure in black tailored suit which she wore with bold black eye makeup as she posed with Halsey ahead of the show

'Makeup has always been something I've just been naturally drawn to because I'm very dexterous with a brush and I know a lot about color,' she explained to Byrdie at the time. 

While further discussing the line, the star stated, 'I really hope that people know that I've always done my own makeup, so this is coming from a place of a lot of experience, and a lot of love for the art.' 

'I meticulously designed all of these products. Everything from the ingredients to the names of every single one of the products—it was entirely of my own love, and design, and approval, and involvement.' 

'I feel like I never really see makeup anymore that's about being f***ed up and dirty and grungy and just wrong, and so much makeup used to be about that,' Halsey expressed, adding, 'I'm just paying homage to the style of makeup and the makeup influences that I've always loved: very '90s-heavy, very Myspace scene queen, very emo revival.'
