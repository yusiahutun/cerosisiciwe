John Bishop slammed for 'awful' jokes after cheeky Carol Vorderman sex life gag

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, March 24, 2024

TV viewers have accused John Bishop of telling “awful” jokes after an instalment of his show that included a cheeky pop at Carol Vorderman.

The comedian started off his ITV programme with a light-hearted take on some of the week’s news, and had a joke about reports that Carol had said she had five “special friends”.

“Five lovers at the same time... ongoing relationships and I tell you what, Carol, that is some Countdown,” he apparently teased.
