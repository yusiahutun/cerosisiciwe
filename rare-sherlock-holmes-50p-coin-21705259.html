Rare Sherlock Holmes 50p coin selling for 6k on eBay and you could have one

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, April 5, 2024

A seller on eBay is flogging an “extremely rare” Sherlock Holmes 50p coin for a whopping £6,815.

The rare coin was created to mark the 160th birthday of Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, author of the detective series, who was born in 1859.

It first came into circulation back in 2019 and they are immensely popular among coin collectors.

Despite the seller on eBay claiming it’s “extremely rare”, it is believed that there are 3.4 million coins in circulation.

So far the person flogging the expensive coin has had 15 watchers and 4 views per hour in time of writing.

The 50p coins can be bought from The Royal Mint Experience or on the Royal Mint website for £10 including a presentation pack.
