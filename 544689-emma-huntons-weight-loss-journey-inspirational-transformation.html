Emma recently made headlines for her impeccable role in Good Trouble and her weight loss journey. Fans noticed that the acclaimed actress looks amazingly different physically than when she first appeared in season one.

After Emma's drastic weight loss within three years, her comment sections have been characterized by compliments about her amazing body transformation. However, besides people being impressed by the actress' body changes, many want to know how she achieved it.

Ironically, Davia Moss, the character played by Emma in the Good Trouble series, was an overweight influencer who received a backlash from her followers when she tried to lose weight. In season 4, Davia deals with negative criticism for losing weight, with followers commenting that she shouldn't have changed.

How did Emma Hunton lose weight?

Since childhood, Emma has been seen as an overweight girl, which made her body conscious. The actress had to face some negative experiences, which marked the start of her weight loss journey.

However, in her X post, Emma revealed that her weight loss decision was personal and not just to meet her character's needs in the Good Trouble show. Another reason for her weight loss, as revealed on April 20, 2021, was partly attributed to her divorce from Ryan.

Emma's view on Davia's role in Good Trouble

In an interview with Buzz, Emma revealed that she rarely sees an overweight girl being portrayed as happy and attractive rather than just being made fun of for her appearance. The role of Davia Moss brings a positive twist to such scenes.

Emma credits the writers for bringing such a positive light to the character of Davia. Her decision to pursue burlesque dancing proves that she's feeling more confident in her skin than ever. She shuts down her roommates' concerns about retreating to her old diet habits by letting them know she is making healthier nutritional choices.
