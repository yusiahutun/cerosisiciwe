Meghan Markle 'looking forward to being treated by Harry' for intimate birthday

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 14, 2024

Meghan Markle is celebrating her 42nd birthday on August 4 and the royals are expected to honor the occasion privately after the recent flood of rumors.

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry‘s break-up rumors have been swirling in recent months but the royals have shut down the speculation by heading for date night on Wednesday ahead of the Duchess’ 42nd birthday.

For the official occasion, the couple are expected to celebrate with a quiet night in, likely to escape the chaotic paparazzi scene in California – here’s everything we know about it.

Meghan Markle’s 42nd birthday expected to be ‘private affair’ with Prince Harry

On Wednesday, August 2, Meghan and Harry celebrated the family birthday at celeb hotspot Tre Lune, an Italian bistro who boasts Kourtney Kardashian and Justin Bieber as customers.

The birthday girl wore a flattering strapless black and white dress, while Prince Harry kept it casual in a blue shirt and light trousers. They were joined by a group of friends, including Meghan’s longtime friend, Heather Dorak, and her husband Matt Cohen, both of who attended their 2018 wedding.

Having celebrated with pals at the upscale restaurant, the royals are expected to spend Meghan’s official birthday at their sprawling $21 million Montecito mansion.

The 16-bedroom estate is a sanctuary for the couple, particularly since Meghan admitted that they did “everything they could to get the house.”

“Because you walk in and go…joy. And exhale. And calm. It’s healing. You feel free,” she gushed.

A PR expert told The Mirror that Meghan’s big day is expected to “be quite toned down this year and likely to be a private affair. I imagine she is looking forward to celebrations and being treated by Prince Harry.”

She continued: “It’s quite possible that Meghan will spend the day of her actual birthday at home in Montecito, surrounded by her two children, Prince Harry, possibly her mother Doria and their rescue dogs. Meghan may also choose to celebrate her special day with a small, intimate gathering.”

The Duke and Duchess of Sussex ‘not included’ in Queen Elizabeth’s death anniversary

It’s no secret that the couple has become increasingly distant from their family since their decision step back as senior members of the British royal family, but tension seemingly escalated after the flood of revelations from Harry’s 2023 memoir, Spare.

Passages recalled Prince William and Harry’s sibling quarrels, which once allegedly resulted in a physical altercation that saw the younger royal “grabbed by the collar and knocked to the floor.”

It seems the tension has continued as Harry and Meghan reportedly did not receive an invitation for a royal family gathering to mark Queen Elizabeth II’s first death anniversary.

King Charles and Queen Camilla will commemorate the anniversary on September 8 at ­Balmoral Castle, Scotland but there has reportedly been “no outreach” to the US-based Sussex royals.
