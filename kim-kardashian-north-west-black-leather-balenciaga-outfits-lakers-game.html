Kim Kardashian and North Match Black Leather Outfits for Lakers Game

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

North West may be following in dad Kanye West’s footsteps when it comes to her music career, but her love of fashion and beauty is all mom Kim Kardashian.

The 10-year-old joined her famous mother for a fun night out Tuesday to watch the Los Angeles Lakers play the Golden State Warriors (who ended up winning, 134–120). The duo sat courtside on Celebrity Row at Los Angeles’s Crypto.com Arena, alongside Kardashian’s girlfriend, retired NBA star Peter Cornell, and actor Will Ferrell.

For the occasion, Kardashian and North matched in all-black looks and leather coats—the Skims founder opting for a long belted trench with golden hardware, and her daughter going for a slouchy, oversize biker jacket with a hood.

Allen Berezovsky//Getty Images

Kardashian—a Balenciaga ambassador—wore her trench with black Balenciaga knife pantaleggings and the brand’s silver mirror hourglass handbag. Her stylish oldest daughter paired her own jacket with black loose pants with white stitching, which pooled around her feet, black sneakers also with white stitching, and Balenciaga’s black netted bag embellished with rhinestones—a piece that retails for nearly $11,000.

Miss Westie finished off her cool look with a fun hairdo: a slicked-down, side-parted topknot with her baby hairs styled into a swirl on one side. She wore glowy makeup and a glossy lip, while Kardashian wore a bronze smoky eye and contour, as well as a glossy brown lip. The reality star too wore her hair in a side-parted ’do.
