In the late 80s, Pat also ran for President. To show people his work he straight away took some major agendas which involved banning abortion and pornography, restoring prayer in public school, ending secularism, and also denying civil rights to homosexuals. Currently, he runs some large companies, including the Christian Coalition, a Christian Right organization that exists to raise monetary and public support for conservative political candidates.
