Jeopardy! fans lose their cool after champ Cris Pannullo misses question by giving 'best possible' w

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, March 9, 2024

JEOPARDY! contestant Cris Pannullo has sent fans into a frenzy after he made a major mix-up on a recent episode.

The game show champ has been on a significant winning streak knocking out his opponents for 20 straight victories, but a recent answer of his turned heads.

The New Jersey native hasn't had a perfect run behind the podium as he recently suffered a major flub on air.

During the November 28th episode, Cris was challenged with an $800 question about fashion.

The question read: "Christian Louboutin sells Lady Page nail polish for $50 a bottle; Vernis from this other Christian runs $30."

Cris was quick on his feet with an answer, replying: "Soriano."

After host Ken Jennings revealed his answer was incorrect, his opponent, Xianni Brown, immediately buzzed in with the correct answer, "Dior."

Fans were shocked that the Jeopardy! powerhouse mixed up Christian Soriano, the soccer star, with Christian Dior, the fashion icon.

However, the customer success operations manager soon recovered and pulled out another win over Xianni and their other opponent Michael Harten.

Cris raked in nearly $40,000 that episode, while Xianni walked away with $2,000, and Michael earned $1,000.

Despite his impressive track record, the games haven't always been smooth sailing for the 38-year-old.

Rob Lamanna, a new player, celebrated audibly when Cris missed a question during the show, missing the chance to take an even bigger lead by snagging a Daily Double.

Out of all of Cris' many wins, 15 have been runaways, meaning there was no chance other players could catch up to him by the time they reached Final Jeopardy.
