Fifi Box addresses recent AFL sexting scandal and reveals she was cheated on

While addressing the recent AFL 'sexting' scandal on her FOX FM101.9 breakfast show on Tuesday, the 38-year-old radio host claimed she had been in a 'similar situation' to the player's current partners.
