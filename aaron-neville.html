Biography

Aaron Neville was born in New Orleans, LA on January 24, 1941. R&B and soul singer who sang with his brothers Charles and Cyril in the Neville Brothers and had four top twenty hits. He also collaborated with Linda Ronstadt on her Triple Platinum 1989 album Cry Like a Rainstorm, Howl Like the Wind, singing duets with Ronstadt on the popular tracks “Don’t Know Much” and “All My Life.” His first major hit single, “Tell It Like It Is,” was later remade and featured Nancy Wilson. He practiced and performed with his brothers from an early age. His song “Over You” was his first release.
