13 of the best cut-out swimsuits for a sexy getaway

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, January 1, 0001

Once considered the bikini's conservative sister, swimsuits have slowly become more stylish over the years and are now arguably the chicest thing to wear to the beach, pool or spa. Exhibit A: the cut-out one-piece. Offering more coverage than a bikini but showing a little more skin than a regular swimming costume, it hits the swimwear sweet spot.

Often seen on the runway and the most stylish celebrities, you'll find lots of cut-out swimsuits to buy online from designers like Zimmermann to high street brands River Island and Pretty Little Thing. Don't know where to start? We've rounded up 13 of the best available to shop for summer 2023.

  • Beach Riot Celine Shine Cut-Out One Piece

    Beach Riot's Celine swimsuit is a bestseller thanks to its super flattering cut, giving the illusion of a smaller waist. Available in a range of shades, we love this golden colourway.

  • ASOS DESIGN Ruched Cut-Out Swimsuit

    Black doesn't have to mean boring, as demonstrated by this ASOS swimsuit. It's cut with a halterneck and high leg.

  • Bikini Bible GiGi Cut-Out One Piece

    Looking for swimwear that's stylish and sustainable? We're obsessed with Bikini Bible's cut-out one piece that's available in several gorgeous colourways.

  • Pretty Little Thing Hot Pink Cut-Out Swimsuit

    If you like your swimsuits bold and bright, you'll love this hot pink one-piece from Pretty Little Thing.

  • Zimmermann Devi Cut-Out Ruffled Swimsuit

    Zimmermann's cut-out ruffle swimsuit is almost too nice to sunbathe in. Almost.

  • Boden Wrap Buckle Cut-Out Swimsuit

    Boden's beautiful white swimsuit is designed to sculpt and smooth your figure. It features a buckle as well as front and back cut-out details.

  • H&M High-Leg Cut-Out Swimsuit

    H&M's bandeau swimsuit features cut-out waist detail, removable padding and a high leg.

  • River Island Animal Print Cut-Out Swimsuit

    Zebra print is always stylish as far as we're concerned and we love this River Island one piece.

  • AllSaints Cara Cut-Out Swimsuit

    Fully lined with hidden support, AllSaints' cut-out Cara swimsuit would look amazing on every figure.

  • Watercult Exotic Dive Swimsuit

    For a subtle cut-out, try Watercult's vibrant swimsuit which also features a sweetheart neckline and lace-up front.

    The delicate spaghetti straps can be adjustable, and worn as a halterneck or cross back.

  • White Fox Kauai Cut-Out One Piece

    Love a skimpy white swimsuit? This is for you.

  • Seafolly Take Flight Cut-Out Swimsuit

    Seafolly's butterfly wing print cut-out swimsuit features frill detailing, removable padding and adjustable ties.

  • SHEIN Cut-Out Swimsuit

    This sultry swimsuit from the SHEIN x Emily Miller collection is perfect for summer beach parties and beyond. Pair with wedges and a wide sun hat to complete your look.
