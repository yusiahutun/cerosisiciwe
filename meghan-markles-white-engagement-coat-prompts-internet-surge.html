Meghan Markle's white coat - engagement Line The Label coat

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

Updated story 1/12: The sell-out white coat worn by Meghan Markle when she announced her engagement to Prince Harry will be available to buy again from February.

The white wrap coat by Canadian brand Line The Label sold out within minutes of the soon-to-be royal wearing it at Monday's photocall. A spokesperson for the company told us that the hugely popular style is currently being re-cut and is expected to land in stores and online from mid-February.

The label's co-founder and president, John Muscat, has already renamed the piece 'the Meghan' in the actress' honour.

Updated story 28/11: The white coat coat worn by Meghan Markle to publicly announce her engagement to Prince Harry has now sold out - although it will be restocked. Created by Canadian brand Line The Label, the piece sparked a huge surge to the company's website forcing it to crash.

The $750 coat consequently sold out "within minutes", but the label's co-founder and president, John Muscat, said the business should be able to meet customer demand.

“We’re not a huge company and it’s the end of the season so we don’t stock a lot of these coats," he told WWD. "They are all handmade.”

Muscat added that the coat has been renamed the 'Meghan' in celebration of the soon-to-be royal. He said he "surprised" and "touched" that she chose to wear the brand.

"She’s bringing a little bit of Canada with her to London,” Muscat said. “She particularly loves this coat. She has it in every colour so that makes a little sense. She wears it like a second skin.”
