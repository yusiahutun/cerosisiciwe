Cody Christian biography

The actor was born on April 15th, 1995, in Portland, Marine, USA. He is the son of Scott Bohall (father), a US Army veteran, and Ashley Bohall. His mother is a native American, while his father is Caucasian white.

The actor's mother is a breast cancer survivor. When she was diagnosed with the disease in 2014, her son, Cody, staged a fundraising campaign towards breast cancer research. His mother is also passionate about raising breast cancer awareness.

Cody was a huge fan of sports and bodybuilding during his early days. He considered taking up a career in that line, but fate had other plans for him. After relocating to Los Angeles, he shifted his interest to the entertainment industry to pursue his dream of becoming a movie star.
