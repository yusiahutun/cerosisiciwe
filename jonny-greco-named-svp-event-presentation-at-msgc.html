Jonny Greco Named SVP Event Presentation At MSGC

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, March 29, 2024

Greco (MSGC)

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — The Madison Square Garden Company has named Jonny Greco to serve as senior vice president and executive producer, event presentation for the New York Rangers, New York Knicks and Westchester Knicks, effective as of November 14.

As senior vice president, Mr. Greco will be responsible for creating in-game and venue entertainment events for MSG.

Mr. Greco joins MSG after a two-year stint as chief experience officer with the NHL’s Vegas Golden Knights. While with the Golden Knights, Greco played a key role in the launch of the team’s inaugural season as a part of the NHL.

Prior to joining the Golden Knights in 2017, Mr. Greco was a senior live event producer at WWE for 6 years.

“Jonny is well known as one of the best in the industry for being an innovative producer and authentic leader,” said Kristin Bernert, senior vice president, business operations, MSG Sports. “He brings a wealth of knowledge on how to enhance the fan experience to this role. We’re thrilled to have someone of his caliber on board and are excited to see what he will bring to The Garden on both the court and ice.”
