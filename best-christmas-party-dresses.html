The best Christmas party dresses 2023

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 19, 2024

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Paco Rabanne

Paco Rabanne

Now 28% Off

If the party season could be captured in one dress, it would be this. Paco Rabanne's iconic sequin chain-disc dress will have you feeling like a disco ball and, naturally, the life and soul of the party. To take it to the next level, wear with the house's matching bag: no one will shine brighter.

Sleeper

Sleeper

Now 40% Off

We can't get enough of Sleeper's playful slip dress thanks to its elegant silhouette and thin shoulder straps. Choose from baby blue, classic black, pale pink, green, navy and a chic chocolatey brown. In Sleeper's signature style, the party dress features a fun feather trim perfect for dancing.

Silvia Astore

Silvia Astore

Is there anything that says party season better than red? Dress to impress with this offering from Silvia Astore – perfect for those occasions when the dress code calls for all-out glamour. Its off-the-shoulder neckline and structured waist create an effortless feel, suggesting elegance rather than Christmas cracker.

Team with simple black strappy heels and let the dress do all the talking. After all, a party dress is forever.

The Vampire's Wife

The Vampire's Wife

Now 40% Off

There's something special about The Vampire's Wife. Founder Susie Cave is known for her vintage-inspired designs that effortlessly balance romance with wearability – worn by everyone from Keira Knightley to Cate Blanchett – and she truly understands what women look and feel confident in. This signature dress with shoulder pads and delicate embellished tulle is the perfect example.

Rotate Birger Christensen

Rotate Birger Christensen

No one does partywear quite like Rotate Birger Christensen. Pay homage to Eighties power silhouettes with this playful purple number, complete with an oversized bow and head-to-toe shimmer. Team it with your go-to party heels for that extra impact.

Taller Marmo

Taller Marmo

Now 50% Off

Partywear edits are often packed with mini hemlines, but Taller Marmo's floor-skimming silhouette offers an elegant alternative. This peachy pink one-shoulder gown will drape beautifully from your frame and is a guaranteed mood booster. Style it with statement heels and wear your hair gracefully pinned up to show off the chic neckline.

Self-Portrait

Self-Portrait

Self-Portrait knows that no festive dress is complete without a little bit of sparkle. We love the timeless style of this design, with crystal-embellished seams that catch the light effortlessly. If you want to push your look even further, add an embellished headband for a quirky spin.

Rixo

Renting your party wardrobe is a great solution if you want to try out a different brand, style or trend without committing too much. If you know you'll still love wearing it year after year then it's a smart investment piece, but if you want to try something out, opting for rental is a smart option that's better for the planet.

Coach

When it comes to party dressing, we love to go all out for the Christmas period until New Year's Eve. Look no further than this high-neck black sequin number by Coach; we guarantee you'll be dancing all party season long and beyond in this number.

Galvan

Another great rental option for party season is this sequinned green slip dress from Galvan, which is currently available on Loan The Look. The company has a great selection of partywear, and even offers a 'try at home service' for London clients where you can select up to five pieces to be delivered to you within a 30-minute window, and then you can decide which ones you would like to rent.

16Arlington

16Arlington

16Arlington's silver sequin dress is here to help you celebrate the season in style. Thanks to its timeless aesthetic, this dress is the ideal investment, not only for party season but for any dressy events you have throughout the year.

Kika Vargas

Kika Vargas

Why not swap the little black dress for a little blue dress this Christmas? We love this voluminous taffeta option from Kika Vargas, which would look great teamed with kitten heels and an updo.
