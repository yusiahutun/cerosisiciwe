Top 40 Country Songs November 2011

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 22, 2024

Our list of the Top 40 Country Songs in November 2011 -- based on chart position, staff reviews and reader input -- features artists that will have a huge month, like Miranda Lambert. The country superstar releases her fourth studio album, 'Four the Record,' on Nov. 1, and her current single, 'Baggage Claim,' is poised to top the charts before Thanksgiving. However, that wasn't enough to put her on top of this month's Top 40 Country Songs list.

Luke Bryan and Chris Young also have two of the hottest songs in country music, as both 'I Don't Want This Night to End' and 'You' are selling singles faster than anyone in Nashville. Well, almost anyone. The hottest song on this list is topping the sales charts, Billboard charts and fan charts. OK, there isn't a fan chart, but there has been little criticism about this song since it was released in July. Congratulations to Blake Shelton, whose 'God Gave Me You' is the hottest song in country music for the second straight month on the Taste of Country chart.
