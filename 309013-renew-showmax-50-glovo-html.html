Renew Showmax and get 50% off with Glovo

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, April 10, 2024

African streaming giant Showmax has partnered with Nairobi’s favourite delivery app Glovo to give away meal vouchers to the first 20,000 subscribers who stack up the Showmax offer four times before July 31, 2019.

Yes, we are rewarding new and existing Showmax customers with a tasty treat including our favourite things: entertainment and 50% off food!

All you need to do is simply pay KSh 250 for Showmax for three months using MPesa, stack this offer up four times and get Showmax for a full year for just KSh 1 000 and win.

The promo offer which commenced on June 18, 2019, will go on until the rewards run out and not beyond July 31, 2019.

How to enter

Buy three months of Showmax for KSh 250 using MPesa (Not sure how to do this? Here’s how)

Stack up the offer by making the same purchase four times in a row and get Showmax for a year.

The first 20,000 subscribers to do so 31 July 31, 2019, will receive 50% off their next food order with Glovo.

How to activate your Glovo voucher

  • If you are a winner, you will be notified by SMS within 24 hours of stacking up on the KSh 250 offer.
  • You will receive a Glovo voucher through a link over SMS. Click the provided link.
  • If you do not have the Glovo app, the link will take you to the App Store to download the app. Download the app, sign up and you will be redirected to the promo code section where the promo code will be auto-filled and applied.
  • If you have the app, you will be redirected to the promo code section of the app where the promo code will be auto-filled and applied.
  • Select the Food category and proceed to make your order.

    • It could not be easier to cash in on a year’s worth of unlimited entertainment from Showmax plus a tasty meal delivered right to your couch, for only KSh 1 000!
