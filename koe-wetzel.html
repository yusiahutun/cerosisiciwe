Koe Wetzel- Biography

Koe Wetzel was born on 14 July 1992 in Texas. Singer and guitarist known as the leader of the country music group Koe Wetzel and the Convicts. The band released their debut album Out on Parole in 2015 before releasing their breakout hit, Noise Complaint, in 2017. He covered the Jason Aldean song “The Truth.” He lived in Pittsburg, Texas, before moving to Stephenville. He’s been to Texas Southern University.
