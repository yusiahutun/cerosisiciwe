So, as Southern Charm Season 9 premiered on 14 September 2023, it also introduced Peacock viewers to Jarrett Thomas one of the three newcomers of the season. (Others are Rodrigo Reyes and Rod Razavi.)

Jarrett, as they say on TV, is a Virginia native and a true Southern gentleman. He is also known for his incredibly successful real estate business and as someone who is really into the partying scene there. Keep reading and we shall tell you more.

Jarrett Thomas On Southern Charm

How Much Is Jarrett Thomas’s Net Worth?

Jarrett Thomass reportedly had above $3 million net worth as of September 2023.

When not socializing with his friends and hitting the town, Jarrett gets himself to his booming real estate business. ​​As part of this job, he had been managing The Inns in Charleston, which are luxury vacation rental homes in downtown Charleston. He is actually the founder of Inns which on its IG @theinns included 22.3K followers at this point. They claim that their mission is to deliver the highest level of luxury vacation rental experience to all of their guests no matter what city they are visiting.
