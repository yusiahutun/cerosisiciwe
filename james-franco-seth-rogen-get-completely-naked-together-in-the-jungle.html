James Franco and Seth Rogen Do &quot;Naked and Afraid&quot; -- And It's Hilarious!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 28, 2024

James Franco and Seth Rogen are known for their crazy antics, and this latest stunt is no exception!

The "Spring Breakers" star took to Instagram to share a series of naked pictures with his on-screen BFF.

"See if me and seth can make it, NAKED and AFRAID!!!! Wtf?! You'll see........" he captioned one photo, referring to Discovery's show, "Naked and Afraid."

The two are sporting necklaces made with large wooden beads and burlap messenger bags, which is the standard uniform for contestants, who are dropped in the wilderness and told to survive without food, water or clothes.

"Boom boom, NAKED and Afraid. Me and Seth, in the bush, naked, together. For reals," the actor wrote.

toofab reached out to Discovery to see if they were actually filming an episode of the popular show -- but we were told they can't comment at this time. Sound like a yes to us!

Clearly, there's nothing James and Seth won't do together!

They recently stripped down to celebrate Jimmy Fallon's birthday on "The Tonight Show," after spoofing Kanye West and Kim Kardashian's music video for "Bound 2."

We're guessing this will be timed to the release of their new movie, "The Interview," but we'll have to wait and see!
