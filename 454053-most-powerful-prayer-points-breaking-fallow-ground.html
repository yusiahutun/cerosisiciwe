Top 15 most powerful prayers on breaking the fallow ground

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 29, 2024

The mind of man is often compared to the ground in the Bible. Therefore, breaking up the fallow ground is to break up your hearts and prepare your minds to bring fruit unto God. The most powerful prayers on breaking the fallow ground help Christians gain a state that is fitted to receive the word of God.

God listens to his people's cries. If you are at a point where you feel that you are stagnating, do not forget to talk to God, as he knows how to lift you. Pray for your success, and ask God to plant and harvest crops on that land you thought would be bare. Read scripture on breaking the fallow ground and live virtuously.

Prayers on breaking the fallow ground

There are many verses on how to break up your fallow ground in KJV. Hosea 10:12, 2 Kings 19:29, and Isaiah 37:30 are relevant scripture. Believers often prepare to sow in a good land when talking about the fallow ground.
