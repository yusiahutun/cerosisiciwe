Janet Jackson's UK Tour Cancelled

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, October 5, 2024

LONDON (CelebrityAccess) — Janet Jackson has gone from coining the term ‘wardrobe malfunction’ to becoming the queen of cancellations. The latest dates to be postponed were all her UK tour dates.


No new dates have been announced for the Unbreakable World Tour and ticket holders are being refunded.
Ticketholders for the shows received an email from Ticketmaster, to say the tour had been postponed “due to scheduling difficulties.” The email also promises a refund to their credit card within 15 days.
Janet has yet to comment of the cancellation of the UK tour.


Fans posted their disappointment via twitter.


In December Miss Jackson took to social media to announce postponing world tour dates while she underwent medical treatment – but confirmed that the European leg of her world tour would go forward.


Janet “Remember … believe it when you hear it from my lips.


“The rumours are untrue. I do not have cancer. I’m recovering.


“My doctors have approved my concerts as scheduled in Europe and, as I promised, the postponed shows will be rescheduled.”


She added: “Thank you for your prayers and love.”
