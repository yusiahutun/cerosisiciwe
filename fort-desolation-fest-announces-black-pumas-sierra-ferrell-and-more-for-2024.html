Fort Desolation Fest Announces Black Pumas, Sierra Ferrell And More For 2024

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 4, 2024

TORREY, UT (CelebrityAccess) – Get ready to immerse yourself in the unique blend of music and nature as Fort Desolation Fest returns triumphantly to the picturesque red rocks of Torrey, UT, from June 6 – 8. This boutique festival promises three unforgettable nights featuring headliners Black Pumas, Sierra Ferrell, and Paul Cauthen alongside a lineup that spans genres and rising stars with The Record Company, Americana favorite Jaime Wyatt, The Lil Smokies and many more.

While the festival is a celebration of music, it goes beyond with an emphasis on outdoor adventure. Located at the Cougar Ridge Resort, just a stone’s throw away from Capitol Reef National Park, Fort Desolation Fest showcases the enchanting beauty of Utah’s “Mighty Five” national parks. Nightly performances allow attendees to spend their days exploring the breathtaking red rock cliffs, canyons, and arches of southern Utah. Outdoor enthusiasts can indulge in fly-fishing, cycling, off-roading, and cruising the Capitol Reef Scenic Drive.

In line with this commitment, Fort Desolation Fest has expanded its camping options for 2024. The campgrounds, known for after-hours jams and campsite cook-offs, will now include the new Sidewinder Campground, a short walk from the main stage. Excitingly, the festival introduces a glamping option, offering a luxurious camping experience in Scandinavian-style tents. Glampers will have access to a concierge lounge, baggage assistance, parking, a morning coffee and pastry bar, and more.

Tickets and camping spots for Fort Desolation Fest 2024 go on sale to the public on Friday (February 2) at noon ET via the festival website. For those eager to be part of the action, local and traveling volunteers can exchange their time for a music pass and a spot in volunteer camping by reaching out to info@fortdesolation.com for more information.
