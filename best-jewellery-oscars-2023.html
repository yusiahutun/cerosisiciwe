The 10 most dazzling jewellery moments at the 2023 Oscars

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, March 31, 2024

The 95th Academy Awards played host to the most stunning array of baubles

    If the Met Gala is the SuperBowl of fashion then the Oscars is certainly the World Cup final. Taking place this year on  Sunday, March 12, 2023, the event is considered the highlight of the awards season calendar. So naturally it plays host to the most extravagant dresses and most stunning make up looks... and of course it’s also all about the jewellery. 

    Back in the early years of the Academy Awards, stars would wear their own baubles or in exceptional cases, borrow them from their movie sets... but now household names from the likes of Van Cleef & Arpels and De Beers clamour to adorn the wrists, necks and lobes of the world’s biggest stars.

    RELATED: Oscars 2023 red carpet fashion: All the jaw-dropping looks not to be missed

    This year everyone was going gaga for green as emeralds seemed to have caught the hearts of the glitterati… Being the magpies that we are, we have put together a round up of the 10 most extravagant jewellery we saw at the 2023 Oscars:

    Sofia Carson

    Performing on the evening, which was nominated for Best Original Song, actress and singer Sofia Carson glittered at the 95th Annual Academy Awards wearing jewellery by Chopard. Sofia selected a necklace featuring seven octagonal shaped emeralds totaling 122.49-carats along with 92.57-carats of pear-shaped, marquise cut, baguette cut and round shaped diamonds set in Fairmined-certified white gold jewellery from the brand's “Haute Joaillerie Collection.”

    Michelle Yeoh

    Oscar nominee Michelle Yeoh looked heavenly in a medley of diamonds, proving they really are a girl's best friend. She donned diamond drop earrings, a diamond tiara and even a dazzling diamond encrusted watch. 

    Angela Bassett 

    Angela Bassett “did the thing” in a stunning Bulgari Serpenti necklace which coiled dramtically around her nape, and paired it with a matching Serpenti bangle from the Roman jewellerys.

    Vanessa Hudgens

    Vanessa Hudgens dazzled in Chanel fine jewellery to compliment her gown from the luxury French maison, styled by Jason Bolden

    Florence Pugh

    Florence Pugh wore a striking modern necklace to accessorise her Valentino couture gown, styled by Rebecca Corbin Murray.

    Emily Blunt

    Emily Blunt attended the 95th Annual Academy Awards wearing pink drop earrings by Chopard, which added a pop of colour to her ensemble - a sleek minimal white gown.

    Fan Bingbing

    Actress Fan Bingbing dazzled in square cut emerald earrings which paired magnificently with the taffeta green sleeves of her dramatic gown. 

    Cara Delvingne 

    Cara Delevingne opted for Bulgari Serpenti jewellery, wrapped around in a high choker and paired with Bulgari earrings.

    Jessica Chastain

    Jessica Chastain opted for Gucci fine jewellery at the 2023 Oscars, wearing an emerald and diamond necklace (cementing the fact that emeralds seem to be everywhere right now.)

    Zoe Saldana

    Zoe Saldana echoed the vintage theme of her Fendi dress in an art deco necklace, which harked back to the roaring 20s.
