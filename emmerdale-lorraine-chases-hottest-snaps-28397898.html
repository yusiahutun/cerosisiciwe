Emmerdale Lorraine Chase's hottest snaps - jungle bod, bikini glam and ageless beauty

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, April 9, 2024

Former Emmerdale star Lorraine Chase is a screen legend.

After years of stardom, the 71-year-old actress joined the ITV soap as Steph Stokes in 2002.

She later bowed out from the show in November 2006 when her character was jailed for life but returned in 2013 to attend the funeral of on-screen father Alan Turner (Richard Thorp).

Lorraine also featured on the 2011 series of I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here!.

16 years on from her Emmerdale exit, Daily Star takes a look inside her hottest ever snaps.

Modelling heyday

Lorraine was born in South-east London and initially earned her living as a model.

The cockney beauty was notorious for her lengthy black hair and in a modelling snap from 1979, a then 28-year-old Lorraine can be seen sporting an incandescent glow and lengthy pins.

The 70s were a pivotal decade for the rising star, who received national attention when she starred in a famous TV ad for alcohol brand Campari.

Thus, a star was born.

Rock chick

Lorraine pulled out all the stops while attending the We Will Rock You premiere at the Dominion Theatre in London in 2002.

Fresh from making her Emmerdale debut, the then 51-year-old star stunned onlookers by wearing a corseted leather dress with a thick gold cross on a chain around her neck.

She wore her glamorous locks straight over her shoulders while posing for photos on the event's red carpet, finishing her look with a black-and-gold chainmail shoulder bag.

Jungle bod

Five years on her from Emmerdale exit, Lorraine joined the cast of I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here!.

The actress featured on the 2011 run of the hit ITV reality series and got pulses racing by showcasing her phenomenal figure in a teeny purple bikini around the camp.

Lorraine featured on the series alongside Mark Wright, Antony Cotton, Fatima Whitbread and eventual king of the jungle Dougie Poynter.

Naut-ical

Lorraine has maintained a quieter life away from the public eye over recent years but in a Twitter post from July 2020, proved she's still game for a laugh.

The former model was all smiles as she took a boat ride alongside her friend Samuel, later posing in a sailor hat.

She captioned the post: "Mucking about on the river with my dear friend Samuel @sambloggs borrowed his ‘at!! [sic]".

Evening glam

Lorraine has debuted a number of stunning looks over the years and during a charity event in November 2012, looked every inch a star.

The stunning actress looked radiant at The Butterfly Ball at the Lancaster Hotel, London - which she attended 10 years ago.

Wearing her hair in a rare ponytail, Lorraine wore large pendant earrings and a black floor-length gown.

She completed her look with a glam makeup palette.
