Alexa Hamilton is an American actress. She appeared as a guest star in the fifth episode of the series The Greatest American Hero, "Saturday on Sunset Boulevard", as the Italian heiress Theresa Chimerosa (or Valenkov), in 1981. Her first starring role on American television was in her late teens, as Sandy Martinson in The Invisible Woman, an unsold television pilot broadcast over NBC as a Sunday night telefilm in 1983. She went on to star and guest star in many TV movies and series throughout the 1980s and early 1990s.
