Early Life

Theodora Petalas possesses a captivating and diverse background that showcases her rich cultural heritage. Born in London in 1995, she hails from a multicultural lineage, with a German mother and a Greek father who is a prominent businessman. Notably, her family boasts a significant connection to the royal house of the Netherlands through ancestral ties, further enhancing her impressive lineage.

One pivotal moment in her life was her prominent role in the wedding of Prince Willem-Alexander of the Netherlands. In the city of Delft on April 24, 2004, when Petalas was just nine years old, she served as a bridesmaid, leaving a lasting impact on her life. Theodora Petalas Career

Per her LinkedIn profile, she began her career as a sales intern (May 2015 – Jul 2015) at Mary Katrantzou, based in London, United Kingdom. She worked as a P.R. intern at Oscar de la Renta for the next three months. Theodora had to cooperate and manage the publications with Vogue and Vanity Fair to increase brand media exposure. She has also worked with companies like You live (Sep 2017 – Sep 2018) and Outfly (Sep 2021 – Feb 2022). Most recently, she has worked as a Research Analyst at In-Trend L.T.D. since Feb 2022 and as a Marketing Communications Specialist at “More Than Health AG” since Jul 2022.
