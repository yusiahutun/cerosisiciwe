1920s Themed Party Ideas - Summer Party Tips

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, March 9, 2024
1

Novel Inspiration

Ben Lozovsky/Simon & Schuster U.K.

"I love to read and turn to good books for ideas and inspiration all the time. The joyful decadence of the parties of the 1920s, like those in F. Scott Fitzgerald's novel The Beautiful and Damned are everything. It's poetic, romantic, and tragic all at once, and you can't help but become immersed in the glamour and lifestyle of the time."

The Beautiful and Damned, $8, amazon.com.

2

Fitzgerald-Inspired Cocktails

Ben Lozovsky

"I love to serve cocktails that feel old-fashioned with a modern-twist. St-Germain Elderflower liqueur is great for summer, and is incredibly versatile for easy at-home mixing with any base spirit. Personally, I love St-Germain and Champagne, so for my signature Maison St-Germain cocktail, we decided to elevate the combination by adding a touch of bitterness. It’s amber colored and topped with gold dust."

3

Floral Frenzy

Ben Lozovsky

"The floral creations from flower artist Brittany Asch of BRRCH are so creative and outside of the norm. I was excited to see how she would work with the surrealist theme for the party, and she did not disappoint. She fashioned vignettes throughout the space with a gorgeous, strange, and avant-garde touch."

4

DJ Spin

Ben Lozovsky

"Having a DJ set by one of your closest friends always takes the party to another level. Andrew VanWyngarden is the greatest and had guests dancing all night long."

5

Surrealist Bites

Ben Lozovsky

"We used a Dali cookbook to inspire surrealist bites for the night. It's truly one of the strangest, most amusing, gorgeous books I’ve seen in a while, and was so fun to bring some of it to life."

Dalí: Les Dîners de Gala, $41, amazon.com.

SHOP NOW

6

Party Attire

Ben Lozovsky

"I think floral dresses are pretty much always appropriate in summer. I love the idea of an easy caftan, short or long for a host. You can serve and cook and run around barefoot and be very comfortable. Same goes for a slip dress—you just can't go wrong. For guests, I’m obsessed with this new line La DoubleJ. The prints are really fun and the shapes are flattering and classic."
