4 Picturesque Places for a Destination Wedding in Switzerland

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, October 2, 2024

If Switzerland’s iconic lakes, meadows, and mountains speak to your style of wanderlust, a ceremony in the Engadin won’t disappoint. With an above-average number of sunny days, good weather is all but guaranteed, and with wedding locations like Muottas Muragl, the end result will be nothing short of a fairytale. This romantic hotel is reached by a vintage funicular railway dating back to 1907 and offers awe-inducing views from over 8,000 feet in elevation. For couples who want a church ceremony, options abound. Eglise au Bois in St. Moritz is a French-speaking church that was built in the late 19th century and boasts a striking wooden open roof truss. The church of San Gian is another beloved local venue; a 14th-century structure featuring a hand-painted wooden ceiling. With seemingly countless picturesque options, a planner with extensive knowledge of the area is a must. If you’re ready to say “I do” in the Engadin, Flurina Caviezel (aka the “PinkFairy”) is your contact.
