Erik ten Hag wears suave suit while watching Man United

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, March 11, 2024

New Manchester United boss Erik ten Hag has been spotted arriving at Selhurst Park in a suave suit for the Red Devils' clash with Crystal Palace.

Ten Hag will be in the stands before he officially takes the reins over the summer. As a result, Ralf Rangnick will be in the dugout before moving into a consultancy role at the club alongside his duties in Austria.

It will be the first time Ten Hag has seen his new side play live. And United will be looking to impress their new gaffer after a less than impressive campaign that has seen the Red Devils slump down the table.

Ten Hag has been joined by former England boss Steve McClaren and Mitchell van der Gaag on Sunday afternoon.

The Dutchman served as his assistant manager at FC Twente for a season, before moving on to work under Pep Guardiola at Bayern Munich. And it's expected for McClaren to join the coaching staff at United over the summer.

Ten Hag was mobbed by fans as he arrived at the gates of Selhurst Park.
