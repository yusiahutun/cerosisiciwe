Brit TV star and ex-policeman Ant Anstead visits his first station

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 30, 2024

Wheelers Dealers host and former policeman Ant Anstead felt reflective on Monday, as he visited his former station in Bishops Stortford. 

The TV star, 42, who is dating Hollywood megastar Renée Zellweger, took to Instagram to share images from his stroll around the Hertfordshire town alongside four throwback snaps from his time on the force.  

He added a caption reading: 'WOW! Trip down memory lane while strolling around Bishops Stortford with the kids I had to stop by the police station I was first stationed at 22 years ago! Gosh I have some crazy stories about that town….. x'.  

Before his TV career, Ant joined his local police force when he was 18 and became one of the youngest members of the country’s Tactical Firearms Unit, based in Welwyn Garden City. He received two commendations for bravery during his career. 

Proud: Wheelers Dealers host and former policeman Ant Anstead felt reflective on Monday, as he visited his former station in Bishops Stortford

Ant beamed from ear to ear in the duo of snaps from his visit to his old stomping grounds, as he posed beneath the police station sign and in the door. 

Clearly feeling nostalgic, the TV star penned his caption about the 'crazy stories' from his time on the force.

Ant has spoken in the past about his former career, as he previously detailed: ‘I was in Her Majesty’s police service for six years. I knew I didn’t want to go down the education route with university etc...

'So I ended up becoming a copper, and I can honestly say it was the best decision I ever made. It forced me to grow up quickly . . . I saw and did things that people would shudder at.’

He certainly did. One of his tasks as a police officer was to help guard notorious serial rapist and killer John Duffy — one of the railway murderers who attacked lone women in southern England in the 1980s — at a safe house.

He’s also spoken of a number of ‘life-threatening incidents’ that ‘really opened my eyes to what humans are capable of’.

When you’re standing in front of someone armed — that’s real fear — it kind of makes standing on stage or in front of a camera easy.’

His transition from British bobby to Californian TV personality is worthy of a movie storyline in itself: in 2005 he was stabbed while apprehending a violent offender and the brush with death caused him to re-evaluate his life. He left the police force and began to pursue his passion for restoring cars.

An honour: He received two commendations for bravery during his career

Proud: Before his TV career, Ant joined his local police force when he was 18 and became one of the youngest members of the country’s Tactical Firearms Unit, based in Welwyn Garden City

He set up shop in a farmer’s derelict cow shed, renovating the place in lieu of a year’s rent, and funded his growing business by playing semi-professional football, amassing more than 700 games in 17 years.

After three years working at the farm alone, his business took off and he assembled a team of car builders. His company began to build bespoke cars for clients all over the world.

As Ant’s reputation as a master mechanic and car designer grew, he attracted the attention of television networks. After initially turning down an offer to appear on British television, Ant co-hosted Channel 4’s For The Love Of Cars in 2013.

In 2017 came the call that took him to California. He was invited to join the hugely popular Wheeler Dealers show which was co-hosted by his London-based car dealer friend Mike, and which has become the No 1 most distributed show on the Discovery Channel, making unlikely stars of the pair.

Hard at work: He was seen in the images working with firearms

However, his burgeoning career took its toll on his marriage to first wife Louise, whom he wed in 2005 and with whom he has two children, Amelie, 17, and 14-year-old Archie. They divorced in 2017, and she and the children still live in Hertfordshire.

‘His first marriage broke down under the pressure of work and him being away from home so much,’ says Mike. ‘It broke him when he split up with Lou...

'It absolutely destroyed him because he was such a family man. He still came to work every day, but he was a mess and we all had to look after him.’

He found love again with American reality TV star Christina Haack. She was separated from her husband Tarek El Moussa, the father of her two children and her co-host on the home renovation show Flip Or Flop.

Sweet: Ant and Renee met while filming her episode of his new series Celebrity IOU Joyride back in June, and have been head-over-heels with one another ever since

She recalled it was love at first sight when they met in late 2017, gushing on Instagram: ‘You take me for what I am, flawed and a bit crazy, and love all of me just the way I am.’

The TV star was equally effusive, calling her ‘the most amazing lady I’ve ever met.’

The pair married in a surprise wedding at their home in California and had a son, Hudson, now two, but by September 2020 it was all over.

Ant and Renee met while filming her episode of his new series Celebrity IOU Joyride back in June, and have been head-over-heels with one another ever since. 

The couple recently went Instagram official, with Ant promoting his new series as he posted a photo of himself with Renee and co-host Cristy Lee on the set of the show.
