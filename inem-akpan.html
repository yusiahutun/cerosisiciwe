She is one of the four children born to her mother Mfon Akpan and a father, whose name has not been given. Inem was brought up along with two brothers namely Okon Jimmy Akpan and Okon Akpan and one sister namely Mfoniso Akpan. Inem is a graduate student of Fiorello H.
