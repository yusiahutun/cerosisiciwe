Jay Manuel Bio, Wiki, Net Worth, Height &amp; Weight

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

The Reality show has also become a phenomenon in the modern media world. The show is a platform for several upcoming promising stars to show their skills and talent to the world. Well, in today’s topic we will be talking about the former creative director of the reality TV show American’s Next Top Model and he is none other than Jay Manuel.

Canadian-American TV host Jay is also known as the renowned makeup artist and stylist for Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, Marie Claire, Victoria’s Secret, Revlon, and Cover Girl. Let us explore his net worth, biography, as well as his personal life in this article.

Jay Manuel’s Bio & Wiki

The native of Springfield, Oregon, Jay Manuel was born on 14 August 1972 in Illinois, Canada under the birth sign Leo. As per his nationality, he is Canadian and belongs to mixed ethnicity.

As per his academic qualification, he joined Dr. Norman Bethune Collegiate Institute. Later, he graduated from York University.

Manuel began his career as a make-up artist with clients such as Tyra Banks and David Bowie. Similarly, in September 2006 he launched his own make-up-line, Manuel Override on QVC. Moreover, he served as a creative director of photo shoots on ‘America’s Next Top Model‘ from 2003 to 2012. However, he was fired from the show and after that, he appeared as the lead judge & host in ‘Canada’s Next Top Model‘.

Furthermore, he also made a guest appearance in ‘Degrassi Takes Manhattan‘. Jay is also known as the renowned makeup artist and stylist for Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, Marie Claire, Victoria’s Secret, Revlon, and Cover Girl.
