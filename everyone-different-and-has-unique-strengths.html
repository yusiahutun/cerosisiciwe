Everyone is different and has unique strengths

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, April 29, 2024

Then I realise the world actually is fair. Everyone has their own characteristics and abilities. Everyone has the same chance to succeed in their own way.

If you think you are weaker than others, don't give up. Try your best. Your uniqueness is your greatest strength.

ISSAC CHENG

Thanks for your wonderful letter, Issac. It's true - we all do have our own special abilities.

I believe that we come into this world with a mission - something that we want to achieve. And that our own unique talent will help us reach that goal.

When you are young, that talent or goal can be quite visible. You know what it is you want to achieve and are not afraid to express it. As people grow older, they may become disillusioned or bogged down in responsibility and social obligation. They forget about their dream.

I hope that you will all see your dream, keep it in your mind and, ultimately, achieve it.

Kate, Editor
