Is Fortnite based on a true story? Everything you should know

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

Fortnite has continued to take the world by storm since its release. The game is trendy, bizarre, and filled with impressive cosmetics. But is Fortnite based on a true story?

Fortnite is an exciting game showcasing the events of a mysterious island. The island is full of combatants, fighting to be the last team or person left standing. With each new season of the game, the plot gets better and more interesting with themes such as time travel and the existence of the multiverse.

Is Fortnite based on a true story?

No, it is not based on a true story. Even though the game offers some imaginative stuff, some people think it's based on real-life occurrences. However, the only common phenomenon between Fortnite and real-world battles is gun fights and paratroopers.

Rumours about the game being inspired by a true story have been making rounds on the internet. People on different social media platforms have posted memes to prove how ridiculous the game's story has become. While it was all meant to be a joke, some have believed the meme, thus alleging that the game is based on a true story.

Fortnite is a fictional story featuring several characters from different fiction works. Originally, it was established to compete against a grittier realistic take on the battle royale genre known as PUBG. Nonetheless, it also drew inspiration from a highly-acclaimed movie, Battle Royale.

What is the game Fortnite based on?

The game was inspired by the dozens of Battle Royale games that have been in existence for years. The Fortnite genre is a familiar idea considering the existence of games such as COD: Warzone, PlayerUnknown’s Battleground, and the BR. In addition, movies such as The Condemned (2007) and Battle Royal (2000) have also been in existence.

The representatives of Fortnite revealed that Minecraft was another huge source of inspiration for their game. The idea of building Fortnite was derived from Minecraft and this heavily influenced its functioning in saving the world.

Even though many people have fun and pass time playing Fortnite, the reason for being a looper is to become the winner while also building statistics. Therefore, pushing others out of bounds and the sick headshots are no reason to make anyone feel guilty about playing. After all, it is just a fictional game.

What was Fortnite initially supposed to be?

Originally, the game was mapped to be a more horror-centric action. According to the art director of the game, Pete Ellis, it was not supposed to be the goofy battle royale it has turned out to be today.

Even though horror-centric action can still be seen through characters like The Husk, things have been steadily changing. With new developments, creators have turned the game into a more cartoonish and family-appropriate style.

Fortnite fast facts

  • What does Fortnite mean? Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released to the public in 2017.
  • Is Fortnite based on the hunger games? No. Fortnite is not based on hunger games. The first hunger games book was released in 2008, drawing inspiration from Battle Royale, a Japanese book and film.
  • Is Fortnite based on a real place? No. Fortnite is not based on a real place. Even though there are real-world gun fights, the place is completely fictional.
  • What was Fortnite's original name? Fortnite's original name was Save the World before receiving funding from microtransactions and transitioning to a free-to-play model.
  • What does OG mean in Fortnite? Fortnite means original gangster. This term shows respect to players who have played the game since the early days.
  • Who made Fortnite famous? Ninja made Fortnite famous during Chapter 1 of the OG era. He is one of the most influential content creators and streamers in the gaming world.
  • What does AFK stand for in Fortnite? AFK stands for Away From Keyboard. It serves as a notice to other players that you will be offline or away from your computer.
