Taylor Lautner and Olivia Holt head to LA church together

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, March 14, 2024

He became a teen heartthrob after flaunting his chiseled physique as werewolf Jacob Black in the Twilight saga.

The Scream Queens alum, 25, and the Disney star, 20, looked low-key as they rolled up to meet with a group of peers for weekday worship.
