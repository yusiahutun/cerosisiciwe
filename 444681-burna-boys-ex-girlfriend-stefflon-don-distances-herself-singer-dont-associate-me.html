Burna Boy's Ex-Girlfriend Stefflon Don Distances Herself from Singer: &quot;Don't Associate Me&quot;

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 30, 2024
  • Stefflon Don, the estranged girlfriend of Nigerian music superstar Burna Boy has got fans talking on social media
  • In what appeared to be a dig at the singer, Stefflon advised her followers not to associate her with anyone she left in the past
  • She also stressed that she is learning to be happy and focus on herself, with many netizens saying the jab was aimed at her ex

Burna Boy's lover, Stefflon Don, has shared a piece of advice for her followers on social media as she revealed the intention to focus on herself.

The British rapper urged her fans not to associate her with anybody in her past as she remained focused on herself.

She shared the plea on her Twitter page.

"Please don’t associate me with anybody I left in the past. Living life and learning to be 100% happy."
