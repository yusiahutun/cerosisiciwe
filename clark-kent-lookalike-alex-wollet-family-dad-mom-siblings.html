Meet Alex Wollet Dad, Robert Wollet

Robert “Bobby” Wollet is 56 years old in 2022. He was born in March 1966.

Robert is currently serving as the Senior Vice President of Commercial & Institutional Banking at PNC. For 22 years before that, he served the company in the role of Vice President of Commercial Banking. His LinkedIn states that he started his career working for Chase as a Fraud/Risk Specialist.

After working in that role for a year, Robert became Financial Analyst from 1990 to 1993. Then for a year, he served as a commercial banking management trainee.

Robert graduated from Youngstown State University in 1989 with Major in Finance and Minor in Economics.

Robert was born to Carole “Sis” Ann Wollet and Harry “Buzz” Wollet. His parents married for 58 years until Carole’s passing on 22 November 2016. Besides Bobby, they shared other three children. They are  Gary (Mary Ann) Wollet of Canfield, Lisa (Robert) Satterlee of Austintown, and Nancy (Edward) Seeco of Boardman.

Carole and Harry had nine grandchildren including Alex.

Moreover, Carole was born on Dec. 22, 1938, in Youngstown and was the daughter of Andrew and Helen (Kunkel) Hutera. She was a 1956 graduate of Chaney High School and retired from the General Electric Lamp Plant in 1995 after 20+ years of service. It was likely his grandmother Carole who inspired Alex to become a doctor.
