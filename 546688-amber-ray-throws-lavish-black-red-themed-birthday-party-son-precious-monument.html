Amber Ray Throws Lavish Black and Red Themed Birthday Party for Son: &quot;My Precious Monument&quot;

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, April 30, 2024
  • Socialite Amber Ray has celebrated her eldest son Gavin's birthday in style, throwing a lavish party for him
  • The black-themed party was decorated with red roses, black balloons and seats and two giant posters of the 14-year-old boy
  • Previously, Amber and her lover Kennedy Rapudo penned heartwarming messages for Gavin, expressing their love for him

Socialite Amber Ray went all out for her son Gavin’s 14th birthday.

How did Amber Ray celebrate Gavin's birthday?

In a series of videos on her Instagram, Amber organised a lavish black and red-themed bash for the 14-year-old.

In one clip, she was seen walking into the party venue, which was decorated with black decor, including balloons, seats and tables. There were red roses and gold napkins on the tables.

The mother of two was dressed in a figure-hugging black dress and was pleased with what she saw when she walked into the venue.

There were two giant posters of Gavin and several lovely backdrops with Happy Birthday written on them.

Another clip shared by Amber showed the birthday boy being picked up by a classy limo.

The boy dressed in a red shirt and white trousers looked dapper as he entered the luxurious car before being ferried to the party venue.

The stylish vehicle was stocked with personalised water bottles bearing his name.

Upon arrival, Gavin was met with a red carpet leading him to the celebration hall.

He couldn't hide his excitement when he saw the preparations his mother had made for him.

What did Amber Ray get Gavin for his birthday?

Another clip shows Amber giving Gavin his birthday gifts.

She presented the boy with an iPhone 15 Pro Max and an Apple Watch Ultra 2, to his delight.

Amber captioned the clip:

"Gifting my son shine ✨ a pair of brand new iPhone 15 pro max and Apple Watch Ultra 2.""@gavinclassic you’re my precious monument and you deserve everything good in this world."

Amber Ray, Kennedy Rapudo birthday messages to Gavin

On April 26, businessman Kennedy Rapudo celebrated his stepson Gavin with a heartwarming message.

He disclosed that Gavin meant a lot to him and appreciated him for being a good child.

The businessman said Gavin had brought joy and happiness to his and the entire family's lives.

On her end, Gavin's mother, Amber, also sent him her best wishes and expressed her deep love for him.

She said she was proud of the remarkable young man Gavin was becoming.
