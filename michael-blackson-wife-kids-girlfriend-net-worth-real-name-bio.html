Michael Blackson Wife, Kids, Girlfriend, Net Worth, Real Name, Bio

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Michael Blackson, the self-proclaimed African king of comedy, is an American comedian of Ghanaian descent, considered one of the most original stand-up comics in the industry. The comedian, who has also done some acting and starred in films such as Next Friday (2000) and Meet the Blacks (2016), has proven to be quite versatile in entertainment and is someone to watch out for.

Michael Blackson Biography (Real Name)

Not much is known about Michael Blackson before he came to the United States at the young age of 13, except that he was born in Ghana on November 28, 1972. There is also no information about his birth, real name, or the identity of his parents.

When he arrived in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, as an African teenager in the 1980s, Blackson found himself the butt of his classmates’ jokes when he arrived in the 1980s because he was African and spoke differently. Blackson said the jokes offended him at first, but later he grew a thick skin that allowed him to use his accent to tell jokes to anyone who was willing to listen. One of the people he told a joke to was an acting coach at Philadelphia Community College, who encouraged him to take acting classes as he was pursuing a career in comedy.

Michael Blackson soon began appearing in a number of nightclubs and bars, before later entering well-known comedy competitions such as the Comedy Central Tour in 1992, the Schlitz Malt Liquor Comedy Tour in Valley Forge, Pennsylvania following year, and the Urban Comedy Festival in New York City in 1996. Soon after, Blackson began appearing on BET’s ComicView series and HBO’s Def Comedy Jam.

His big break came after one of these shows, which featured Ice Cube. The former rapper, who became an actor and film producer, was in the audience and witnessed one of Blackson’s performances in his usual African clothing. This led him to offer the comedian the role of an angry African in the comedy Next Friday (2000), and the rest became history.

Michael Blackson’s Net Worth

Even though Michael Blackson may not be one of the best-paid comedians out there, he is very well off with a net worth of allegedly around $2 million. The comedian, who has appeared in more than seven films to date, most notably next Friday, where he plays an angry African, has amassed most of his wealth not only through his film roles but also by directing a series of live shows at comedy clubs, universities and other special events in and outside the United States.

Blackson also has a huge YouTube fan base, where views can generate considerable income.
