Margot Robbie Is Pretty in Little Pastel Pink Dress Suit From Chanel

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 25, 2024

At this point, Margot Robbie’s name has almost become synonymous with Barbie, and it can be hard for us to imagine her not wearing pink on the red carpet. But if it ain’t broke, don’t fix it.

On Monday, the actor and producer attended the Oscars Nominees Luncheon at the Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles, where she wore a little matching dress suit (in pink, of course) from Chanel.

Michael Buckner//Getty Images

This time, though, while Robbie did decide to honor her Best Picture–nominated film with her fit, she didn’t opt for hot pink, but a more subtle shade. Less Barbiecore, more Barbie Lite.

The Bombshell star’s matching set included a cropped jacket left unbuttoned to reveal a bandeau top and a tiered high-waisted miniskirt underneath. All three pieces were made of a tweed cream material with hints of pastel pink throughout and very light diamond-shaped stitching in purple. Robbie also carried a top-handle bag from Chanel made of the exact same material, in the same pattern. To round out the ensemble, she sported custom ecru heels with a black capped-toe detail from Manolo Blahnik.

Michael Buckner//Getty Images

Michael Buckner//Getty Images

While Robbie’s attendance at the nominees luncheon might come as a surprise (given that she was snubbed in the acting category), the Australia native is a nominee as a producer on Barbie. (The Oscar for Best Picture always goes to the producers of the winning film.)

Recently, Robbie departed from Barbiecore when she wore a black floor-length gown from Celine to the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences’ 14th Governors Awards in January. She also wore a black plunging blazer dress to W Magazine’s Annual Best Performances Party in the same month. Only time will tell how the star will rep her film come Oscars night.
