Mia Thornton is a reality star who happens to be a new face for the Real Housewives of Potomac's season six. Furthermore, she is a businesswoman and mother of Gordon Thornton's three children.

Mia Thornton - Married to Husband Gordon

Mia Thornton has been blissfully married to her husband, Gordon Thornton, and have been together for more than a decade. Mr. and Mrs. Thornton met each other for the first time in 2003 in a strip club. And, the 36-year-old doesn't shy away from flaunting their chemistry whenever she can.

Mia Thornton is married to Gordon Thornton. SOURCE: Instagram.

Before this, Gordon and Mia were friends and served on the same board for a nonprofit organization to fund money for Haiti. Moreover, they have been exclusively together for thirteen years now. Mia tied the knot with Gordon on May 5, 2012.

Mia Thornton shared three little children with her present husband, Gordon Thornton. The couple welcomed their first child Jeremiah Thornton in 2015, followed by Juliana Thornton in July 2017, and Joshua Thornton in 2018.

Mia Thornton is a mother to three kids. SOURCE: Instagram

Surprisingly enough, not biologically but, Mia has step-grandchildren through her husband Gordon, who is 32 years older than the reality star. Mia has four grandbabies, including two adults moving into the college world and two in school.

Update 2024: Mia Thornton Announces Separation from Husband

In a recent development, Real Housewives of Potomac star Mia Thornton and her longtime husband, Gordon Thornton, have decided to end their marriage. After being together for 11 years, the couple officially separated in September 2023. Although allegations of infidelity surfaced, Mia and Gordon opted for a civil divorce settlement.

Since their separation, Mia has embraced a new chapter in her life and is currently in a new relationship, signaling a fresh start after the end of her marriage.

How rich is RHOP cast Mia Thornton?

Mia Thornton has earned a colossal net worth of $5 million with her hard work and dedication. The RHOP cast makes a hefty sum of money from her career as a franchise manager for the Joint Chiropractic Doctor Offices. And, she has already expanded her business in four different states: North Carolina, Maryland, Virginia, and New York.
