M&amp;S 15 Toffee Vodka wins 'Double Gold Medal' at International Spirits Awards

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, February 17, 2024

Marks and Spencer has made some innovative moves of late, delighting customers with new products and offers.

For Wimbledon season, M&S launched pink gin cream, perfect for pouring on strawberries.

Then, the retailer announced an incredible picnic deal especially for summer months.

As well as launching ‘Aperol Spritz’ in a can, their own version being called The Aperitivo Spritzer.

And now, the store has revealed their own-brand Toffee Vodka was one of four spirits and liqueurs that were awarded Double Gold Medals at the ISC awards.

The Toffee Vodka is a rich, toffee-scented vodka, which is perfect over ice, with coca cola or as part of a sweet cocktail.

Not only that, but the Toffee Vodka costs just £15 in UK stores.

Other spirits and liqueurs that won gold were the Slow Gin, Elderflower Liqueur and Raspberry Vodka.

In other M&S news, the store had relaunched its £12 dine in for two meal deal.

The brand has also launched ‘full’ Percy Pig sweets for the first time.
