The 15 Best Wide-Calf Cowboy Boots in 2024

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

Best Traditional Cowboy Boots

Ariat Heritage Western Boot

Best Traditional Cowboy Boots

Ariat Heritage Western Boot

Choose between two different widths for a boot that feels tailored to you. The R-toe offers a traditional look, while a removable cushioned insole enhances comfort for all-day wear.

Sizes: 5.5–11

Colors: Black, distressed brown, copper brown

What reviewers are saying: “Love, love, love these boots. I was worried they wouldn’t be wide enough for my foot or my calves but they fit perfect.”

Best Medium-Height Cowboy Boots

AllSaints Dolly Western Leather Boots

Best Medium-Height Cowboy Boots

AllSaints Dolly Western Leather Boots

The extra-wide shaft of these boots makes them ideal for people with larger calves, and the slight taper to the ankle keeps them fitted. The chunky heel is the brand’s contemporary spin on the classic style.

Sizes: 5–11

Colors: Black

Best White Cowboy Boots

Billini Homestead Wide-Calf Boots

Best White Cowboy Boots

Billini Homestead Wide-Calf Boots

This all-white boot is perfect for warmer weather, pairing great with light-wash denim and pastel tops. Zinn suggests “throwing a tee and blazer on to give a little prep balance to the western boot, and you’re ready to go.”

Sizes: 6–11

Colors: White, black

What reviewers are saying: “It’s hard to find boots that don’t tighten at your knees, and these wide calf boots fit like a dream. If I ever could, I would order more pairs of these boot beauties.”

Best Heeled Cowboy Boots

Thursday Boots Sierra

Best Heeled Cowboy Boots

Thursday Boots Sierra

A stocky shaft is paired with a thick heel for a style that elongates the legs without climbing too high. And the ample colors and styles mean you can choose a boot that feels the most like you. Antimicrobial shock-absorbing insoles add an element of practicality as well.

Sizes: 5–12

Colors: Tan, white, black, tobacco

What reviewers are saying: “These boots are gorgeous and exactly as pictured. Definitely true to size—but if you’re unsure or between sizes, pick a half size down.”

Best Custom Cowboy Boots

Kemo Sabe Kate Boots

Best Custom Cowboy Boots

Kemo Sabe Kate Boots

If you want something truly bespoke, Kemo Sabe might be the place to go. Valdmanis says, “My favorite boot right now is the Kate boot. We designed it with the woman in mind who wants a sexier height (it’s a 16-inch upper, so it hits just below the knee) as well as the 2.5-inch heel height (our tallest heel in the boot line).” She adds that Kemo Sabe creates “custom boots where we take the customer’s exact measurements to ensure absolute precision. When looking for a taller boot with someone who has wider calves, we recommend designing a custom boot with us for the perfect fit.”

Sizes: 6–9.5

Colors: Off-white

Best Range of Cowboy Boots
