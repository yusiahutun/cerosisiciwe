Maxwell Mwamburi Discloses Crushing on Juma Jux, DM'd Him on IG: &quot;He Ignored&quot;

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 8, 2024
  • Maxwell Mwamburi said his father has been angry at him and is yet to talk to him since he announced he's gay, but he doesn't care
  • The businessman also shared he has a huge crush on Tanzanian singer Juma Jux and that he reached out to him on DM
  • He, however, said he is living his truth after pretending for many years, acting like a straight man

Queer businessman Maxwell Mwamburi, now going by Maxine Mkamburi, has opened up about his crush on Tanzanian singer Juma Jux.

Maxwell Mwamburi tells off old dad

In an interview with Mungai Eve, the gay baker said he shot his shot on his DM on Instagram, but the seasoned singer never responded to his message.

"I just told him, 'Hi Juma, when you come to Nairobi, hit me up', but he has never responded. I have been attracted to him since high school. I have been fantasising about the whole thing but hakunijibu, but it's okay, we are getting ignored anyway. I don't care if he's no attracted to men," he said.
