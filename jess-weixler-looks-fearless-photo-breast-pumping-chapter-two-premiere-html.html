Jess Weixler looks fearless in photo of herself breast pumping before IT Chapter Two premiere

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 1, 2024

She's a working mom and she's not afraid to show it.

Jess Weixler, 38, shared one of the less glamorous parts of motherhood as she took to her Instagram to show herself breast pumping while getting ready for the premiere of her new movie, IT Chapter Two on Tuesday. 

The new mom attended the Los Angeles premiere on Monday but made sure fans had a behind-the-scenes look of what it took to get all glammed up.

Talking to Just Jared, the star said she shared to photo to decrease the stigma around nursing and being a working mother.

'There is a lot of stigma around who can and can't breastfeed and going back to work,' she said.
