The Best Way to Scent Your Home With Essential Oils

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, October 6, 2024

Your windows are bolted shut, the heat is on high, and there’s a staleness in the air — welcome to your home come the dead of winter. While extremely common, that lingering odor that you just can’t get rid of during the cold winter months can be frustrating. Airing out your home isn’t always possible this time of year, and factors like fireplaces and heating systems don’t make things any easier. Luckily, we have the perfect life hack that’ll help freshen up your home in minutes — and it only involves two things: your vacuum and your favorite essential oil.  

The Best Way to Scent Your Home

So, what is the essential oil vacuum hack? The idea here is to turn your vacuum into a diffuser. And just how do you diffuse essential oils with a vacuum? Depending on your vacuum, there’s actually a couple ways you can go about this. Some people like to soak a cotton ball with their favorite essential oil and drop it into the dust canister before using it. Others choose to place a couple drops of oil on their vacuum’s filter or collection bag. No matter which method you use, as long as you place some essential oil within your vacuum, you’ll notice results immediately. As you go about vacuuming, the motor will heat up and help diffuse the oil as you move from to room to room, freshening your home as you go and making that stale stench a thing of the past.

Why You Should Use Essential Oils

If you’ve used essential oils before, it’s likely been in an aromatherapeutic setting. However, the benefits of essential oils go way beyond their calming and soothing nature. Many essential oils are also anti-fungal and antibacterial in nature, meaning that they can help clean and sterilize an environment. So, by diffusing them with your vacuum, you’ll be giving your home an especially thorough cleaning with minimal additional effort.
