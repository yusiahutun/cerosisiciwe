Exclusive: Paris' Car Crash and LAPD Encounter on Tape

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 6, 2024

An LAPD official told TMZ that the department is still investigating a car crash involving Paris Hilton. The incident, which TMZ caught on tape, occured at 2:30AM PT Nov. 9. Paris' boyfriend, Greek shipping heir Stavros Niarchos, crashed the heiress' Bentley into a parked truck, then sped away with Paris, Kimberly Stewart (Rod Stewart's daughter) and Talan Torriero, star of MTV's 'Laguna Beach' all inside.
