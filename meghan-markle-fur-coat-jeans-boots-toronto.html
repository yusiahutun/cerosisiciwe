Meghan Markle Wears Chic Fur Coat and Beanie While Shopping in Snowy Toronto

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

Not even a little snow can stop Meghan Markle from getting her Christmas shopping done.

The Suits star was spotted braving the cold with a friend in Toronto while hitting up a series of stores on Sunday afternoon, and by the looks of it her trip was quite successful. For the snowy outing, the gorgeous actress opted for a cozy olive green Smythe coat that featured a fur collar and oversize pockets ($795; nordstrom.com). She paired her chic outerwear with distressed skinny jeans, heeled suede Sarah Flint booties, an emerald green crossbody bag, and a classic black beanie (shop one like it here). Markle wore her dark locks in loose waves as she walked hand-in-hand with her pal through the streets.

We wonder if there's anything in those bags for her boyfriend Prince Harry?
