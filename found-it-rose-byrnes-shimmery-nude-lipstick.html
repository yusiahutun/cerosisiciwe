Found It! Rose Byrne's Shimmery Nude Lipstick

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Rose Byrne’s lipstick at the New York City premiere of Portlandia is a perfect example of a sensual lip with a just-kissed flush, and we love the extra dimension created by the faint metallic finish. “We wanted to enhance Rose’s natural tones with a soft, effortless look that would complement the beautiful vintage dress she was wearing,” said her makeup artist Hung Vanngo, who used CK One’s lipstick in Braniac, a rosy nude with a touch of shimmer. “This shade is a great choice for those who love a nude lip, but still want to have a hint of peach and pink.” Pick up a tube for $16 at ulta.com.
