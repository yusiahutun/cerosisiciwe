Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's Full Relationship Timeline

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 27, 2024

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce confirmed their budding romance in September 2023, when the singer showed up to cheer on the Kansas City Chiefs tight end at a home game at Missouri’s Arrowhead Stadium. The move came months after Kelce first publicly expressed interest in Swift, after attending one of her concerts.

Ahead, see everything we know about their relationship so far.

April 28, 2024: Taylor accompanies Travis to Patrick Mahomes's gala.

Swift plays the girlfriend part as she and Kelce attend friend Patrick Mahomes’s 15 and the Mahomies Vegas Golf Classic and gala weekend. For the gala night, Swift dresses up in a layered golden green dress with spaghetti straps, and gold jewelry. Kelce looks cool in a black suit and white dress shirt with no tie. In a video shared by the Kansas City Chiefs quarterback, Swift and Kelce are seen holding hands while entering the gala.

April 19, 2024: Taylor releases her first love songs about Travis.

On 11th album The Tortured Poets Department, out today, Swift seems to allude to her relationship with Kelce for the first time in her lyrics, on a few songs. Most notably, in “The Alchemy” and bonus track “So High School,” she makes several references to football.

“So when I touch down / Call the amateurs and cut ’em from the team / Ditch the clowns, get the crown / Baby, I’m the one to be,” she sings on “The Alchemy.”

And on “So High School,” she sings, “Truth, dare, spin bottles / You know how to ball, I know Aristotle / Brand new, full throttle.”

April 13, 2024: They enjoy a sweet Coachella date.

Swift and Kelce look so loved-up on their date at the big Southern California music festival, dancing, swaying, singing, holding hands, and kissing as they enjoy several of the acts, including Swift’s friends and collaborators Ice Spice and Jack Antonoff (who fronts the band Bleachers).

Gilbert Flores

To make things even sweeter, the “Karma” singer reps her man with her sleek desert fit, wearing a forest-green baseball cap boasting the name of Kelce’s podcast, New Heights (which he cohosts with his brother, Jason). Swift keeps the rest of her ensemble monochromatic black, wearing a shiny leather jacket over a corset top and pleated skirt, paired with ankle socks and sneakers. She accessorizes with Stella McCartney’s vegan Logo small shoulder bag and the designer’s oversize square sunglasses, wears her blonde tresses down in soft waves, and glams up with her signature black winged eyeliner and red lipstick.

Kelce, meanwhile, oozes cool in white-and-blue striped trousers; a classic white T-shirt; a gray, white, blue, and red flannel; a multicolor handkerchief tied around his neck; white Converse sneakers; and a white baseball cap with “Happy Gilmore” across the front in green lettering. He accessorizes with dark-tinted shades, small hoop earrings, and a chain necklace.

Later in the day, they turn their outing into a double date, meeting up with fellow celebs singer Sabrina Carpenter and actor Barry Keoghan.

April 12, 2024: They hold hands on a date night in L.A.

The superstar couple spends some sweet quality time in Los Angeles. The two are captured walking hand in hand while leaving a dinner date at Sushi Park.

Courtesy of Gucci

Swift gives her signature preppy style a sporty twist, donning a varsity jacket in none other than bright Kansas City red to rep her man. She pairs the white-accented piece with a black top, a denim miniskirt in a dark wash, and black leather ankle boots, and carries the Gucci Luce bag. She wears her golden locks down in soft waves and opts for her signature black winged eyeliner and red lips. Kelce sports a monochrome olive-green ensemble consisting of a knit sweater and shorts.

April 10, 2024: Travis jokes about the start of their relationship.

In a new episode of his New Heights podcast, Kelce and guest Lil Dicky (a.k.a. Dave Burd) talk about the NFL star’s relationship with Swift.

At one point, Burd offers his take on the public fascination surrounding the romance. “I just think there’s something that makes everyone feel like the world is kind of like high school, where your most popular pop star, like, beloved musician somehow met, like, your most popular beloved athlete, and they actually fell in love and it’s real,” Burd says. “I dunno, I think anyone who hates on it is a bitter loser. But I think a lot of people who [you] would maybe expect to hate on it actually love it and acknowledge they love it, because there’s just something so American about it or something. There’s something just classic about it.”

To that, Kelce laughs and jokes: “I don't know how I did it, because she was not into sports. So I don’t know how the fuck I did it.”

April 2, 2024: Travis says he’s never been happier.

“I’m the happiest I’ve ever been. I’m a guy that some people say is glass half full, half empty. And my glass is all the way full,” Kelce tells People in a candid interview. “It’s all the way full.”

Having just won his third Super Bowl with the Chiefs and going strong with Swift since last year, the tight end adds, “I’m oozing life right now.”

April 1, 2024: Kylie Kelce comments on Travis and Taylor’s relationship.

During an appearance on the Today show, Kylie Kelce—wife of Travis’s brother Jason—shares her thoughts on her brother-in-law’s relationship with the pop star.

“Ultimately, if Travis is happy, we’re happy,” Kylie says. “We are always cheering on Uncle Trav—it’s such a treat to be able to do that on and off the field.” She adds of the high-profile romance: “But it’s been amazing.”

January 26, 2024: Travis addresses dealing with public attention.

At a Chiefs press conference, the tight end discusses how he and Swift are maintaining their privacy, with such a spotlight on their relationship.

“As long as we’re happy, we can’t listen to anything that’s outside noise,” Kelce says. “That’s all that matters.”

He goes on to acknowledge the attention he has received over the past year, crediting both Super Bowl LVII in February 2023—in which he faced off and won against his brother and the rest of the Philadelphia Eagles—and his relationship with the pop superstar.

“I brought this upon myself, and I do enjoy having fun with it all, and the biggest thing is making sure my focus is right here in this building,” Kelce says.

“You hear the media throughout the year, if we’re not having success, you know, maybe throw it out there that I wasn’t focused or that the team isn’t focused on certain things, and if you’re in this building, you know exactly what’s going on,” he continues. “So, you gotta compartmentalize what you’re hearing and make sure that you’re staying on task, and giving everybody in the building and on that team the right perception.”

December 6, 2023: Taylor talks about Travis publicly for the first time.

As Time’s 2023 Person of the Year, the Grammy winner opens up to the magazine about her supportive relationship with the NFL player.

She clarifies the timeline of their romance, revealing that the first time she attended a Kansas City Chiefs game was, in fact, not their first date.

“This all started when Travis very adorably put me on blast on his podcast, which I thought was metal as hell,” Swift explains. “We started hanging out right after that. So we actually had a significant amount of time that no one knew, which I’m grateful for, because we got to get to know each other. By the time I went to that first game, we were a couple. I think some people think that they saw our first date at that game? We would never be psychotic enough to hard-launch a first date.”

Although Swift valued their privacy at the start of their relationship, she says the couple has no plans to run from the public. “When you say a relationship is public, that means I’m going to see him do what he loves, we’re showing up for each other, other people are there and we don’t care,” she says. “The opposite of that is, you have to go to an extreme amount of effort to make sure no one knows that you’re seeing someone. And we’re just proud of each other.”

The singer also addresses her viral, well-documented cameos at Chiefs games. “I don’t know how they know what suite I’m in,” she says. “There’s a camera, like, a half mile away, and you don’t know where it is, and you have no idea when the camera is putting you in the broadcast, so I don’t know if I’m being shown 17 times or once.”

Despite the attention her attendance receives, she says, “I’m just there to support Travis. I have no awareness of if I’m being shown too much and pissing off a few dads, Brads, and Chads.”

November 20, 2023: Travis talks about dating Taylor in new interview.

In his December/January cover story for WSJ. Magazine, Kelce opens up for the first time about his relationship with Swift.

Speaking of how they first connected, he says he had some people close to Swift “playing Cupid” for them, after he shot his shot at her Eras Tour. They eventually gave her his contact information, and he “got lucky enough to get her to reach out.”

“She’ll probably hate me for saying this, but … when she came to Arrowhead, they gave her the big locker room as a dressing room, and her little cousins were taking pictures … in front of my locker,” he recalls.

As for their first meeting, Kelce reveals, “When I met her in New York, we had already kind of been talking, so I knew we could have a nice dinner and, like, a conversation, and what goes from there will go from there.”

Kelce also says in the interview that he has “never dated anyone with that kind of aura about them” before the 12-time Grammy winner. “I’ve never dealt with it,” he says. “But at the same time, I’m not running away from any of it … the scrutiny she gets, how much she has a magnifying glass on her, every single day, paparazzi outside her house, outside every restaurant she goes to, after every flight she gets off, and she’s just living, enjoying life. When she acts like that, I better not be the one acting all strange.”

Elsewhere in the interview, Kelce calls his new girlfriend “hilarious” and “a genius,” and reveals that he and the superstar share a similar outlook on family, work, and life. “Everybody knows I’m a family guy,” he says. “Her team is her family. Her family does a lot of stuff in terms of the tour, the marketing, being around, so I think she has a lot of those values as well, which is right up my alley.”
