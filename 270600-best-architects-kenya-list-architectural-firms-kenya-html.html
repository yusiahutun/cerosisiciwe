Association of Architects in Kenya - Architectural Association of Kenya

The Architectural Association of Kenya was found in the year 1967. It is one of the leading associations in Kenya. It is registered under the Societies Act and unites all the professionals from the Academia, Public Sector, and Private Sector. Its main objectives are:

  • To generate revenue for the association.
  • To protect the natural environment and maintain the heritage buildings.
  • It even offers community services by improving the environment, maintaining the information of buildings and carefully inspects the quality of materials.
  • Helps in research for further advancement in training, professional education and practice.
  • Aims at improving the standard of the professional by training, education
  • The association and the public get to know about the built and natural environment, through the published documents.
