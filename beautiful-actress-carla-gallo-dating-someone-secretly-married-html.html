Short Bio of Carla Gallo:

Carla Paolina Gallo was born on 24th June 1975 in Brooklyn, New York. She is said to have graduated from Cornell University with a degree in Theater. She has an estimated Net Worth of $2 million.

In the year 1994, she got her first movie break for the role of Toni Peck in the movie Spanking the monkey while in the year 1999, she played the role of Janet Tuckman for the series”Law&Order.” She came in limelight from the television series Underdeclared(2001-2003), Carnivale(2003-2005), Bones(2008) and Californication(2008-2009).

In the year 2016, she has undertaken the project Neighbours 2: Sorority Rising. In the year 2013-2014, she worked for the television series “Blues” and Anger Management (2014).
