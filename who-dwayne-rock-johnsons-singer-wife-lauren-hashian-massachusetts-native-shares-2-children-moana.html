Lauren Hashian studied PR

According to People, Lauren Hashian grew up in Massachusetts and went on to study public relations and communications at university. Her LinkedIn shows that she worked in the creative licensing department at Warner Music Group from 2008 to 2010, and the motion picture music department at Paramount Pictures from 2010 to 2012.

She is an American musician

Hashian has got back into the music industry recent years, but in a different way, putting herself out there as a musician and singer in her own right – something she never thought she’d do.

“I was too shy of a kid, and I really wasn’t not shy until … I became a mother,” she told Billboard in 2021. “And then I felt way more confident in my own body and my own feelings. It was like, ‘Well, you can’t be scared of anything any more, because you don’t want to teach your kids that, right?’”

When did Dwayne Johnson and Lauren Hashian meet?

According to People, Johnson and Hashian met years ago. They reportedly first locked eyes on the set of Johnson’s The Game Plan film in 2006, then began dating the following year.

Johnson opened up to People about how he felt fortunate to have found someone again. “I was so fortunate to have fallen in love once,” he said. “To fall in love again? That’s a hard thing to do twice in the position I’m in. I’m one lucky son of a b****.”

When did Dwayne Johnson and Lauren Hashian get married?

After being together for over a decade, Johnson and Hashian tied the knot in a “secret” ceremony in Hawaii in August 2019. “We had a full security detail in case there were helicopters,” Johnson told the WSJ Magazine. “No one knew. I was shocked but so grateful. My life is so loud and noisy; the fact that it was quiet was a big win.”

He told the publication that his “divorce did a number on me” and he “wasn’t fearful of getting married again, there was just some hesitancy”.
