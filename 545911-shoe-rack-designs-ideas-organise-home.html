15 shoe rack designs ideas that will help organise your home

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 1, 2024

People spend a fortune on shoes and love to flaunt their collections. But what is an excellent collection without a great shoe rack? Luckily, shoe racks are available in all sizes, shapes, materials, and colours to match your home décor. So, which are the trendiest shoe rack design ideas for your home?

Shoe racks offer versatile solutions for organising footwear while optimising space. From compact shelves to 3-tier racks, they efficiently declutter living spaces, keeping shoes tidy and accessible.

Shoe rack designs in Kenya

When creating this list of shoe rack designs for the home, we considered various factors, including online reviews, customer feedback, and product specifications. We also relied on reputable sources like Furniture Elegance and Jumia to ensure you select the best. Here is a list of the trendiest shoe rack designs for the home.

1. Closet shoe rack

Closet shoe racks help maximise the space in any closet size, reducing clutter. They allow you to store several pairs, sparing the door, floor and hanging space.

These shoe racks are made from various materials, including wood, bamboo, metal, and plastic. They are also available in different styles, such as revolving, tiered, cubby, or stackable.

2. DIY PVC pipe shoe organiser

This budget-friendly design involves cutting PVC pipes into equal lengths, ensuring they're tall enough to hold shoes. The PVC pipes are mounted onto a sturdy backing board or hung directly on a wall. A bracket attaches the PVC pipes to the wall or board for stability. You can customise them with paint or decorative elements to match your décor.

3. 4-tier metallic shoe rack

A 4-tier metallic shoe rack offers a sleek, space-saving solution for organising footwear. Made from durable metal, it features four tiers of open shelves, providing ample storage space for multiple pairs of shoes.

The metallic finish adds a contemporary touch to any room, while the open design allows for easy visibility and shoe access. With its compact footprint, this rack is among the top shoe storage ideas for small spaces.

4. DIY painted shoe bench

A DIY-painted shoe bench is a customised seating and storage solution for entryways or mudrooms. It typically features a bench seat for sitting while putting on or taking off shoes, plus a lower shelf or cubbies for shoe storage. You can personalise it to blend with your space through painting.

5. Pegboard shoe rack

Are you looking for shoe storage ideas for your entryway? The pegboard shoe rack is among the top, budget-friendly shoe storage ideas for entryway.

A pegboard shoe rack comprises a perforated wood board with inserted pegs or hooks at various heights and configurations. Shoes are hung from the pegs or hooks, maximising vertical space and keeping them easily accessible.

6. Shoe storage baskets

Shoe storage baskets are woven or fabric containers designed to organise and store footwear neatly. They are usually made from wicker, rattan, or fabric.

Shoe storage baskets come in various sizes and shapes, accommodating different types of shoes, from sandals to boots. Their open design allows for easy access and ventilation, helping to keep shoes fresh.

7. Industrial DIY shoe rack

This industrial DIY shoe rack has a lovely, rugged charm. Usually constructed from metal pipes, fittings, and reclaimed wood, it exudes a raw and edgy aesthetic.

The design features open shelves or tiers, allowing for easy storage and access to footwear. Its industrial-inspired look adds character to any space while using repurposed materials contributes to sustainability.

8. Two-tier shoe storage bench

This two-tier shoe storage bench features two levels of shelves for storing footwear while providing a convenient place to sit.

9. Over-the-door shoe organiser

An over-the-door shoe organiser is a versatile storage solution that maximises space in closets or bedrooms. Most are made from durable fabric or clear plastic, with multiple pockets or compartments hanging over a door's back. The clear pockets allow for quick identification of footwear.

10. Small space-saving shoe rack

Are you looking for shoe rack designs for small spaces? This shoe rack is a compact and efficient solution that maximises storage in tight spaces. It has multiple tiers or shelves for organising shoes vertically. Its slim profile allows it to fit into narrow spaces such as closets, entryways, etc., making it ideal for small houses or rooms with limited floor space.

11. Wooden shoe bin

The wooden shoe bin is a charming and rustic storage solution made from sturdy wood. It’s usually rectangular or square and has an open top for easy shoe access. The wooden construction will add some charm to your space.

12. Custom shoe drawers

Unlike traditional shoe storage options, these drawers are designed to accommodate footwear, with customisable compartments or dividers to suit different shoe sizes and styles. Their customisable nature allows for creative organisation strategies, such as arranging shoes by colour or type.

13. Adjustable shoe organiser

This design features adjustable shelves or dividers that can be easily repositioned to accommodate different shoe sizes and styles.

14. Shoe cabinet with doors

This cabinet-style design usually has one or more doors. The doors provide a sleek and streamlined look while keeping shoes out of sight and protected from dust.

15. 3-tier wooden shoe rack

This is one of the famous wooden shoe rack designs for home. It has three open shelves, providing ample storage space for multiple pairs of shoes. Its compact design maximises vertical space while maintaining a small footprint, making it ideal for entryways, closets, or bedrooms with limited space.
