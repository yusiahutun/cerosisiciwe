Jedidiah Goodacre, who conquered millions of heart by portraying the character of Jetpack Buddy in the Disney big-budget feature film Tomorrowland, might be a dream boyfriend to many ladies out there.

Goodacre, who is on his way to enjoy immense success and popularity, has been maintaining privacy in his personal life because of which his relationship status might be confusing to many of you.

So is the Prince Chad Charming from the 2015 film Descendants dating someone? Has he found his princess or is he probably single? Well, let us help you to investigate his life behind the cameras!

Goodacre’s Life Behind the cameras

We can’t deny the fact that Jedidiah Goodacre, age 28, is good looking and has an ability to charm people around him. But is he single, or has a girlfriend to accompany him in his success?

When Goodacre’s co-star from Descendants, Booboo Stewart is flaunting his romantic relationship all over the Instagram, Goodacre hasn’t hinted his love life. He has neither talked about it in any of his interviews.

Even if you stalk him in different social networking sites like Twitter and Instagram, you find no any evidence about his love life.

So the question remains, is he single, or is hiding his love interest away from his followers?

Well, if Goodacre is single, this might be good news for ladies out there, who wish to date Goodacre in their real life.

However, some wiki sites suggest Goodacre belong to the gay side, but it's wrong to do so just because of his low-key love life, right? What if he is dating secretly?

Well, though Goodacre hasn’t talked about his girl in social media, what his fans should know is that he is a fun loving person who loves traveling. Here is an Instagram post by him where he shares his picture from hiking.

Well, he also has shared a picture with his friend who seems to be as fun loving and crazy as he is.

We must say that Goodacre has maintained his physique very well. He seems to be working on his acting skills as well as his body.

Aren’t those abs perfect for the guy, who stands tall at the height of five feet ten inches?

You might not know that this white ethnicity actor is very attached to his parents and family. He doesn’t hesitate at all to express his love towards his family in social media.

Last year in September, he shared a picture where he was surrounded with a package of clothes and shoes. In the caption, he said-

“this package of awesome came thanks to my amazing sister @hgoodacre and @vburgt I have been missing my wonderful family so much, I can't wait to see all of you soon ?????”

The Vancouver Film School graduate Jedidiah is now all set to add a new acting credit in the list of the movies that he has appeared. He is one of the casts of the 2017 movie Descendants 2.

Similarly, Goodacre has recently appeared in Some Assembly Required (2016), Supernatural (2016), Dying to Be Loved (2016) and Motive (2016).
