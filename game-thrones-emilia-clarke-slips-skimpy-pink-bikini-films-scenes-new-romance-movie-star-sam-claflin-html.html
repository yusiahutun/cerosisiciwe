Emilia Clarke slips into skimpy pink bikini for Me Before You with Sam Claflin

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, October 8, 2024

While she’s famed for playing Mother of Dragons, Daenerys Targaryen, in the hugely popular Game Of Thrones franchise, Emilia Clarke has been busy gearing up for a completely different role.

The 28-year-old star threw herself into another day of shooting as she joined fellow cast and crew members to film scenes for new movie Me Before You.

The British beauty looked to be in good spirits as she teamed up with co-star Sam Claflin at Formentor beach in Mallorca, on Wednesday afternoon.

The actress - who is playing Louisa Clark from the bestselling book, by Jojo Moyes – took to her Instagram page to share a snap from the film’s idyllic location.

‘Spain there is no rain, and even less to complain... about,’ she captioned the image. ‘#MeBeforeYou filming just keeps getting better!#givemesunshine.’

Atmospheric: Taking in the beautiful surroundings: The 28-year-old was seen sporting a skimpy pink bikini and a half worn diving suit while her dark tresses were left loose in tousled waves

Meanwhile, 28-year-old Sam was confined to a wheelchair, dressed in character with a dark blue polo-shirt and white chinos.

Me Before You tells the tale of a small town lady called Lou Clark who strikes up a friendship with a recently paralyzed man, called Will Traynor. 

The upcoming drama will see Clarke perform alongside her former co-star Game Of Thrones co-star Charles Dance and Harry Potter star Matthew Lewis as well as Doctor Who’s Jenna Coleman.

Elsewhere, Emilia's career has taken off in recent months, with the star taking over the iconic character of Sarah Connor in the Terminator reboot opposite Arnold Schwarzenegger.

But there's nothing the actress likes more than returning to the Game Of Thrones set after a break from filming.

'I went back to GOT and it was like coming home', she told VOGUE. 'Just the smell of the hotel in Belfast made me feel safe. I told myself, forget the big game plan; all I want is to do jobs that make me happy.'

Me Before You recently received its release date and has been slated for June 3, 2016 release.
