Roseanne Barr Falls

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 27, 2024

Getty Images

Actress Roseanne Barr was in San Francisco visiting her son when she had an unexpected accident.

Barr was in Golden Gate Park when she slipped and fell, breaking her kneecap in three places. Fortunately, her sister was with her and she “took control of the situation,” Barr tweeted.
