Stars Share Favorite Halloween Pics!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, March 31, 2024

Happy Halloween! This most hallowed of holidays has luckily fallen on a Thursday this year, so our favorite celebs are sharing some special snapshots of the most memorable costumed moments from the past.

Jimmy Kimmel also got into the Halloween spirit! He posted a pic from his childhood on Facebook with the caption, "Happy Halloween! Jimmy and his sister in Brooklyn, New York - 1976. #ThrowbackThursday #tbt."

Cindy Crawford posted a sexy homage to "Grease" on Twitter with the caption, "#TBT: Rande and I about ten years ago as Danny & Sandy from Grease at Herb Ritts' Halloween party! Happy Halloween!"

We love Nicole Scherzinger's Halloween throwback! She posted a pic on Facebook as a pint-sized cutie with the caption, "Happy Halloween!! Here's a pic of me at 7 years old, dressed up as Carmen Miranda!! Chica Chica Boom Chic!! #TBT"
