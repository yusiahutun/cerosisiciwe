Snapchat ban: How to get your account or device unbanned

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, February 10, 2024

Like any other social media platform, Snapchat moderates its app using a set of terms of service and community guidelines to which every user must adhere. However, violating these terms of service or guidelines can lead to a Snapchat ban. How do you get your account or device unbanned if you fall victim?

Snapchat is a limited-time media-sharing application that has greatly evolved over the years. Initially, it was a one-on-one messaging platform, but now it incorporates numerous features, including sending videos, hosting live chats, and seeing friends on a Snap Map.

What causes you to get banned from Snapchat?

There are numerous reasons why your Snapchat account or device can be banned. Sometimes, you could get wrongly banned due to algorithm mistakes or hacking. However, most people's devices or accounts are banned for legitimate mistakes such as:

  • Sharing illegal or banned content.
  • Bullying other Snapchat users.
  • Creating fake accounts or sharing fake information.
  • When Snapchat suspects that your account has been hacked.
  • Installing third-party Snap apps.
  • When Snapchat thinks you are a robot.

Can you get unbanned from Snapchat?

Yes, it is possible to get unbanned from Snapchat under various circumstances. Presently, appealing a Snapchat ban is the only way one can recover a banned account. Luckily, even if you cannot recover the account, you can create a new one.

One downside to creating another Snapchat account is that failing to understand the process that led to the initial ban can lead to banning subsequent accounts. How long are Snapchat bans? In essence, the type of ban determines how soon you get back.

Types of Snapchat bans

Are Snapchat bans permanent? Some bans are permanent, while others are short-lived. Here is a look at the various punishments for suspected offenders.

  • Temporary bans - You are blocked for a certain time frame.
  • Permanent bans - You are blocked from accessing your Snapchat account unless your appeal is successful.
  • IP address ban - The ban is placed against your internet network, but you can bypass it with a VPN.
  • Device ban - You are blocked from using Snapchat on your device.

How to get your account or device unbanned

There are several ways to unlock Snapchat after getting banned from the app. Here are some of the possible ways:

1. Wait for the ban to expire

If your account has been temporarily banned, waiting for the ban to expire is the best thing. Since Snapchat fingerprints your device's unique IMEI, creating a new account with the same device will get you another ban. However, you can use the same device to create a new account after six months.

2. Appealing your ban

What happens when you get banned on Snapchat? You should appeal to recover your account. Unless you are logged into your Snapchat account, the help section is unavailable. Therefore, you are required to use a mobile browser or desktop and follow the steps below:

  • Visit Snapchat Support and click on the Contact Us option.
  • Click on the "I can't access my account" option.
  • Click on the ' I see an error message when I log in' option.
  • Click 'yes' on the still need help prompt.
  • Fill in the form, including your username and an explanation of what happened.
  • Submit your appeal request.

3. Use a virtual private network (VPN).

Does Snapchat ban IP addresses? Yes. When this happens, a VPN is the simplest way to get unbanned. Your IP address changes when you connect to the internet with a virtual private network.

Websites will see the VPN server's IP address instead of your usual IP address. To get unbanned from Snapchat using a VPN, follow the steps below:

  • Delete Snapchat from your device.
  • Visit NordVPN and create an account.
  • Install NordVPN on the device.
  • Connect to a VPN server, preferably a U.S. or European one.
  • Re-install Snapchat (Don’t log in with your old account).
  • Now, you can access Snapchat and create a new account.

4. Use a dynamic IP address

If you are experiencing a Snapchat device ban, using a dynamic IP address works almost the same as using a VPN to unban your account.

To do this, you only have to restart your router or reset it to factory default. If you use a dynamic IP address, unbanning your account is as simple as rebooting the router.

5. Connect to your phone’s Wi-Fi hotspot

If you are fond of connecting your Snapchat from a desktop device, bypassing the Snapchat IP address ban using the Wi-Fi hotspot option is easy. This is possible for both Android and iPhone users. Here is how to unban Snapchat using the Android hotspot:

  • Open the ‘Settings’ app.
  • Select on the ‘Mobile Hotspot & Tethering’ option.
  • Tap on ‘Mobile Hotspot.’
  • Set up a network with a name and password for security.
  • Connect to the hotspot using the desktop device you usually use for Snapchat.
  • By now, you have access to Snapchat.
  • Create a new account.

Unban Snapchat using an iPhone hotspot by following the procedure below:

  • Open your ‘Utilities’ menu on an iPhone.
  • Long-press the menu that holds Wi-Fi.
  • When it expands, tap on the ‘Personal Hotspot’ option.
  • From the desktop device, connect to the hotspot network.
  • By now, you can access Snapchat.
  • Create a new account without being banned.

6. Reset your device ID to unban Snapchat

If your device has been blocked, resetting it could be the only option to unban Snapchat. Without resetting it, creating a new Snapchat account will be impossible. You can easily reset your Android or iPhone devices by following the steps below:

For Android users, you need to reset the device back to the factory setting through the simple process below:

  • Open ‘Settings’.
  • Tap on the 'General Management' option.
  • Tap on ‘Reset’.
  • Choose ‘Factory Data Reset.
  • Select “Erase All Data.”

Remember, this erases everything, so you must go through the entire setup procedure on your device. However, you can create a new Snapchat account without risking another ban.

Unlike Android users, iPhone users are required to use additional steps to revert to factory default. The process is as simple as:

  • Go to your ‘Settings’ app
  • Tap on the ‘General’ option
  • Scroll and select the 'Reset’ option
  • Tap on the 'Reset All Contents and Settings’ option

How do you avoid getting banned on Snapchat?

To avoid a Snapchat account banning, keep your account clean and comply with the app's terms of service and community guidelines. When creating an account, use a real email address and phone number and verify your information details. Also, be sure to follow these simple rules:

  • Don’t install third-party Snapchat apps.
  • Don’t share your account details with anyone.
  • Keep your password private and secure.
  • Don’t share information that goes against Snapchat community guidelines.
  • Don’t spam, harass, or bully other Snapchat users.

How to fix Snapchat ban - Wrapping up

Snapchat ban is one of the worst experiences Snapchat users go through. However, the good news is a Snapchat account ban is not always permanent. You can successfully get your account or device unbanned or create another account by following a few steps as detailed above.
