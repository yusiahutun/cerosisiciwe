See Which Country Stars Have December Birthdays

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, March 19, 2024

This December, we'll be celebrating Christmas Day, Hanukkah and ringing in the new year â€” but that's not all. Loads of country stars will also be blowing out the candles on their birthday cakes this month!

The festivities begin early, with Jana Kramer blowing out her birthday candles on Dec. 2. Lila McCann celebrates a few days later, along with Diamond Rio's Brian Prout and Chris Hillman of the Byrds. Gary Allan turns another year older on Dec. 5, while our favorite wine lovin' country gal, Sunny Sweeney, will blow out her candles on Dec. 7.

Sam Hunt may celebrate his Dec. 8 birthday with a "House Party," and we just hope we're invited! We can't imagine how fabulously glamorous Meghan Linsey's birthday cake will be a couple of days later, or what Hank Williams III will unwrap on his special day (Dec. 12). Even if she's not making country music these days, we'll still be listening to Taylor Swift songs for her birthday on Dec. 13. Frankie Ballard, Tracy Byrd, Randy Houser and Cowboy Troy also celebrate birthdays in December.
