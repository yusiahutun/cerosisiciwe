Jeff Bezos' lover Lauren Sanchez previously dated NBA's Derek Fisher

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 29, 2024

Jeff Bezos' lover Lauren Sanchez was previously in a relationship with basketball star Derek Fisher after splitting from NFL fiancé Anthony Miller - it has been revealed.

A source told PageSix Sanchez dated the former LA Lakers star on-and-off for a while after her engagement to the Superbowl winner was called off back in 2002.

'Lauren was with Derek Fisher for a while [after] things went south with Anthony Miller,' the source claimed.

'Then she traded up to 'NYPD Blue' star Henry Simmons, and they were engaged. Then she was getting married to Patrick Whitesell.' 

Miller claimed that despite a five-year relationship with Sanchez, he felt he could 'never really trust her'. 

The former NFL star, 53, met Sanchez at the 1996 Super Bowl when he was playing for the Denver Broncos and she was a rising star TV reporter.

The pair were together for four years and engaged for one and a half before Miller says he broke things off.

'I couldn’t really trust her,' Miller told the Mail on Sunday. 'She gets around. She was cheating on me with other guys.

However, sources close to the couple have disputed Miller's claims, saying that it was him who cheated on Sanchez with a stripper.

Those sources also called the athlete an 'opportunist' for speaking out about the relationship two decades later, right after the 49-year-old's alleged eight-month affair with world's richest man Jeff Bezos was revealed.

The pair allegedly began seeing each other while she was still married to talent agent Patrick Whitesell. She was previously engaged to NYPD Blue actor Henry Simmons and has a 17-year-old son with former NFL player Tony Gonzalez. 

Sanchez (far left) was spotted alongside Bezos at Amazon's Golden Globes party on January 6 

The 54-year-old Amazon founder Bezos and wife MacKenzie, 48, announced their split after 25 years of marriage in a joint statement last week.

The announcement appeared to have been forced by the National Enquirer, which had been documenting his affair with Sanchez for months and collected a number of photos and sexy texts between them.

A longtime friend of Sanchez on Monday told DailyMail.com of how the married TV anchor set her sights on Jeff Bezos after she and her talent agent husband Patrick Whitesell became friends with the billionaire several years ago in Seattle.

After meeting Bezos and MacKenzie socially several years ago, Sanchez told a friend: 'Now that's the kind of guy I need,' the close friend claimed.
