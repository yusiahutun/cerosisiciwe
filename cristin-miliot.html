Is The Venture Bros and Modern Love Actress, Cristin Miliot Single Or Dating At The Moment?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, May 30, 2024

One-time Grammy Award-winning American actress and singer Cristin Milioti has successfully established herself as a notable star in the Hollywood entertainment industry. Likewise, she rose to stardom after starring as Tracy McConnell in the CBS sitcom, How I Met Your Mother in season 9 from 2013 until 2014.

Moreover, Cristin is also notable for her recurring role in Broadway theatre production such as That Face, for which she was nominated for Lucille Lortel Award. 

Born on August 16, 1985, she won the hearts of numerous fans with her outstanding acting skills and stunning looks. Her devotees are keen to know about her relationship status. Let’s explore it.

Is Cristin Milioti Married? Or Is She Still Single?

Thirty-six years old, Milioti has been notoriously private regarding her individual life. Consequently, there are less details available regarding her personal life. The lady seems to be a secretive type of person who has successfully kept the borderline between her personal and professional life.

Also, Cristin has kept her lips locked when the matter comes to her lovelife, and she lives a low-key life besides her on-screen career.

Currently, the star has not publicly shared any further details about being in a relationship for several years. So at this point, it’s still a mystery if she’s dating anyone or not. Nevertheless, it is safe to say, the actress is single and is probably waiting for the right guy to fall in love.

Scrolling her IG page, there are no cozy pictures with any guy. Instead, the lady keeps posting photos of herself with her co-stars and related to her job. However, Cristin has been seen in several romantic sitcoms in recent years.

Nowadays, the singer who was raised in Cherry Hill, New Jersey remains completely focused on her proficient career. Even became an immensely popular star due to her incredible performances in television shows and movies. 

Relationship With Ex-Boyfriend, Jesse Hooker

Previously, a Tony Award-nominated actress was linked up with her now ex-lover, Jesse Hooker. Her ex-partner is an actor turned woodworker and is the son of TV actress Lorna Luft and Jake Hooker, a late musician.
