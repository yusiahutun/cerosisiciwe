All the Details on Ronda Rousey's Wedding Gown (Exclusive)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 21, 2024

MMA fighter Ronda Rousey said "I do" over the weekend in Hawaii and TooFab's has all the details on her gorgeous white wedding gown.

Rousey got married to Travis Browne wearing a hand-beaded mermaid dress from designer Galia Lahav, who gave us a little insight into how Ronda picked her look for the big day.

"We collaborated with Ronda and her stylist Alejandro Peraza in order to create the best experience for Ronda, who we adore and admire much," Lahav told TooFab. "Both of them came to our LA flagship store in Hollywood, met with our stylists, and had fittings like every other bride. It didn't take much because they seemed to know exactly what's best for her."

Rousey chose the Harper gown from Lahav's Le Secret Royal bridal couture collection.

"Harper gown is a sexy mermaid dress with a body hugging silhouette, made of scalloped motives, hand beaded lace with a low V-neckline and hand beaded adornment all around the front and back neckline," Lahav added. "We use European blended fabrics, to get to the best figure, high-end look, while remain the gown's comfort."

We're told the design was altered only slightly for Ronda but "was basically just a perfect fit to begin with." The dress cost somewhere in the $10-15k range.

"We get to know some amazing women in this business, and it's an honor to be there for each and every bride. Ronda is humble and beyond gorgeous. You can't really ask for more," the designer added.
