Gypsy Rose Blanchard poses with AEW title as she enjoys 'VIP' treatment after release

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

Gypsy Rose Blanchard has been busy enjoying her newfound freedom as she was recently spotted with a champion wrestler while attending this week's episode of AEW Dynamite as a special guest.

The 32-year-old spent eight years behind bars for her involvement in her mother's murder. Clauddine 'Dee Dee' Blanchard was stabbed to death in her home in Missouri in 2015.

Dee Dee had Munchausen syndrome by proxy, a psychological disorder that sees caregivers fake symptoms or cause real symptoms to make it look like a child is sick for attention. After plotting her mother's murder with her then-boyfriend Nicholas Godejohn, Gypsy was sentenced to 10 years in jail.

Gypsy took to Instagram to share some photos from the show. In one picture she could be seen grinning widely as she held up Ricky's Tag-Team World Champion belt.

Gypsy wore a simple black tank top for the occasion and accessorised with a pair of fishnet fingerless gloves as well as silver face gems. The wrestling star placed his hand on Gypsy's shoulder as he posed alongside her in another photo.

She captioned the post: "Thank you Ricky Stark and AEW Dynamite for inviting us to a amazing show tonight We had a great time!" Gypsy's husband, Ryan Scott Anderson, also joined her at the behind the scenes meeting.

Ryan started writing to Gypsy while she was in jail and the pair quickly struck up a romance. They tied the knot in an intimate prison ceremony in July 2022.

Getting VIP treatment at pro-wrestling events isn't the only fun the spouses have been having since Gypsy's release, she's also revealed they've been busy getting frisky. Gypsy left fans feeling "sick" after boasting about their sex life online.

In response to hateful comments Ryan had been receiving on his Instagram, Gypsy wrote: "They jealous because you are rocking my world every night…yeah I said it, the D is fire happy wife happy life." Ryan responded: "Who said I gave a damn about what these jealous people say anyway, haha...now come get it Baby..."

The X-rated comments left the couple's followers shocked as they dubbed them "freaky" and "cringey". Gypsy was a healthy child, but her mother forced her to use a wheelchair and feeding tube while telling people she suffered from muscular dystrophy, leukaemia and other illnesses.

But in 2012 she met Godejohn on a Christian singles website and they began dating long-distance until 2015 when they plotted to murder Dee Dee. Godejohn stabbed Dee Dee 17 times while she was asleep.

Following the murder, he and Gypsy fled to his home in Wisconsin but police eventually caught up with the pair. Godejohn was convicted of first-degree murder and sentenced to life behind bars while Gypsy was convicted of second-degree murder.
