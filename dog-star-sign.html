12 Dog Star Signs, Their Personality Traits And Human Match

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, March 8, 2024

Do you know what your dog's star sign is? Well, if you follow the astrological calendar, dog's birth dates can be tracked to help you connect with parts of their personality. What's more, some believe you can go on to match certain dog star signs to their perfect human match.

Canine Cottages, with the help of astrologer Emily Thornton, have researched and created a unique birth chart for dogs. Ever wondered why your puppy is quiet and thoughtful, whilst your neighbour's is loud and playful? The stars could be the answer...

What is my dog's star sign?

Aquarius (January 20th - February 18th)

An Aquarius dog is eccentric, clever and independent. They are most compatible with human Geminis. You'll probably never have a dull moment with one of these around – they'll easily learn new tricks, and won't be as needy as many others dogs.

Pisces (February 19th - March 20th)

A Pisces puppy is affectionate, creative and insightful. They are most compatible with human Cancers. These dogs are warm and gentle, and love to cuddle and relax with their owners – that is how they show affection. If you're after a therapy dog, you can't go wrong with a kind Pisces pooch.

Aries (March 21st - April 19th)

An Aries dog is cheerful, lively and confident. They are most compatible with Aquarius humans. These dogs are spirited, being able to run and play for long periods of time. Even so, they love cosying up to a warm fire just as much, and are perfect for humans with active lifestyles.

Taurus (April 20th - May 20th)

Taurus dogs are reliable, committed and a little lazy. They are most compatible with human Virgos. These dogs hate a bath, but still love a roll around in the mud. Over everything else, they are loyal and empathetic, and the best friend an owner could dream of.

Gemini (May 21st - June 20th)

Gemini dogs are inquisitive, resilient and intelligent. They are most compatible with human Libras. Geminis are active dogs that are in need of stimulation often via toys and playful activities. They are amongst some of the brightest dogs, and will quickly learn and retain new tricks that you teach them.

Cancer (June 21st - July 22nd)

Dogs born under the Cancer sign are warm-hearted, protective and friendly. They're constantly being affectionate with their owners, yet are still strong protectors, caring for both owner(s) and children of the family. For this reason, they can make a great guard dog. Along with this, Cancer dogs love a swim, so make sure to take them to the beach whenever possible.

Leo (July 23rd - August 22nd)

Leo dogs are proud attention seekers, and very confident. They are most compatible with Sagittarius humans. Because of their love for attention, don't be surprised if your Leo puppy is stopped in the park or on the street by people wanting to pet it. Leos are charming and give off great energy. Despite being loved by all, they'll still love curling up with their owners the most.

Virgo (August 23rd - September 22nd)

Virgo dogs are organised, smart and kind. They are most compatible with human Capricorns. They may well be one of the most organised of other dogs, and will probably be very particular about who can touch their toys and other belongings. Virgo dogs are very smart. Teach them tricks and play with them, and always remember to tell them how much of a good boy/girl they are - they love that.

Libra (September 23rd - October 22nd)

Libra dogs are social, soft-hearted and non-confrontational. They are most compatible with human Leos. Leos love having other dogs in the family, since they're such socialites. They dislike loud barking and conflict, though, so will usually steer clear if that ever occurs. They also love a dress-up, and shouldn't put up a fuss at all when dressed in puppy outfits.

Scorpio (October 23rd - November 21st)

Scorpio dogs are unique. They are loyal and brave, yet secretive. They will protect you from anything, at any time, and love going on adventures and exploring. Their secretive nature, though, means that they usually cover their tracks if they do something wrong. They're also known to keep grudges, even against other dogs, so it's best to stay on their good side.

Sagittarius (November 22nd - December 21st)

Sagittarius dogs are adventurous, feisty and spontaneous. They are most compatible with Aries humans. Be sure to let your Sagittarius dog run free and explore as much as possible - a vast open space would be ideal, since they love exploring new places and getting their noses stuck in to everything they can. They may be a bit more vocal than other dogs, as they can be feisty, but will nevertheless make as good a companion as any other.

Capricorn (December 22nd - January 19th)

Capricorn dogs are persevering, determined and practical. They are most compatible with Taurus humans. Capricorns make great training dogs due to their persistent nature, and will always work for their food. Expect them to be very active, and be prepared to take them out often. They will always leave a lot of room for love, affection and cuddles, though.
