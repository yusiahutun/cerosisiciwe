'Phenomenal' viral edit of Taylor Swift in Travis Kelce shirt 'looks so real'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

After months of speculation, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are officially an item and now fans are convinced the singer is wearing a shirt on her Eras tour making reference to her other half. From that bracelet to Taylor having the time of her life watching a Chiefs game with his mom, the two have been dishing out adorable moments non-stop since dating rumors began.

At the time of writing, Taylor Swift is in Argentina on her Eras tour. The 22 singer had to reschedule her Buenos Aires performance on November 11 due to “chaotic” weather conditions. But, she confirmed the show will be rescheduled for Sunday, November 12. Throughout her performances this year, Swifties have been picking up on references to her romance during Taylor’s sets.

Taylor Swift ‘wears Travis Kelce shirt’

Taylor had fans’ minds racing when they thought that she was wearing a Chiefs shirt back in July.

As she shared a clip to her TikTok page, the Blank Space singer got eagle-eyed fans thinking that she was teasing her Travis Kelce romance.

Now, a viral clip is doing the rounds on the same social media site. It shows Taylor “wearing” a different shirt.

The short video, from an account called @bradrossmedia, sees Taylor performing on stage wearing a black hat and seemingly a white shirt that reads: “Who is Travis Kelce anyway? Ew.”

‘Editing is phenomenal’

The super real-looking clip has TikTok users double-taking as Taylor genuinely appears to be performing wearing the shirt.

It’s safe to say the Shake it Off performer wasn’t wearing a shirt with that wording on it. But, fans are giving some serious props to whoever created the edit.

Many commented on the six-second clip to ask if it was “real,” and more wrote that the edit was “phenomenal.”

Some said the video made them “question themselves”. More simply wrote how talented they thought the editor was: “The editing on that is insane.”
