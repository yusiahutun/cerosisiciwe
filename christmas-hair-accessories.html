5 Christmas Hair Accessories That Don't Suck

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

If you celebrate it, Christmas is a time of presents, togetherness, ugly sweaters—and even uglier headbands. Or it was, until now: it turns out that there’s more to the Christmas hair accessories game than just felt reindeer antlers. These are 5 Christmas hair accessories that are sleek, chic and most importantly, don’t suck.

Gold Holly Leaf Hair Pins ($13, Etsy)
These chic metallic holly leaves are the perfect way to add a festive touch to you ‘do without looking like Buddy the Elf threw up on you. Each leaf is attached to its own pin, so you can wear them in any configuration you like—we especially love them tucked into the base of a ballerina bun.

Related Stories

18K Gold Plated Shooting Star Hair Clip ($25, Etsy)
Why not go big for Christmas with this gorgeous star hair clip? Perfect in long and short hair alike, it adds the perfect amount of glam to your Silent Night. See what we did there?

Crystal Snowflake Hair Comb ($53, Etsy)
If you want to reference winter wonderland outside while still looking elegant, this beyond-delicate hair accessory from Jana Royale is definitely the move.

Red Flower Headband ($13, Etsy)
If you’re more of a headband babe, this felt flower headband is the way to do it. It’s definitely a statement, so make sure you pair it with a sleek, chic hairstyle for maximum glamour.

Traditional Christmas Bow Headband ($19, Etsy)
And if you want to go big, we say go for it in a bold red reindeer-printed hair bow. It’s retro and kitchy in the best way, especially when paired with a bright lip and winter-white sweater.
