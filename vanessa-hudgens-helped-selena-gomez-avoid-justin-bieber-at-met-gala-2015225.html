Vanessa Hudgens Helped Selena Gomez Avoid Justin Bieber at Met Gala?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 3, 2024

Selena Gomez has a quality girlfriend in Vanessa Hudgens! The High School Musical alum appeared on Watch What Happens Live on Thursday, May 21, where she was asked whether she was an accomplice in recently helping Gomez avoid Justin Bieber at the 2015 Met Gala.

“Getting spicy now are we?” the Broadway star, 26, replied while playing coy about the subject. “Um… I mean, not really.”

Hudgens had a very rational perspective about Gomez and Bieber’s off-again, on-again romantic history. “She’s one of my best friends and you just want to have your girlfriends’ backs,” Hudgens mused of her Spring Breakers costar. “So that’s what I did. We didn’t really try to avoid him or anything. I mean, no one really wants to see your ex boyfriend.”

Bieber, however, was more forthcoming about being in the same room with Gomez. “Selena looked gorgeous at the Met Ball!” the “Baby” hitmaker told TMZ the morning after the gala earlier this month.

As previously reported by Us Weekly, Hudgens, Gomez, Bieber, and a slew of other stars hit the swanky afterparties following the Gala on May 5. At the Standard Hotel — where Solange memorably attacked her brother-in-law Jay Z in an elevator after the 2014 Met Gala — the former Disney actresses mingled with Jessica Alba, Reese Witherspoon, and Kerry Washington.
