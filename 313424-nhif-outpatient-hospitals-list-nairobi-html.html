List of accredited NHIF outpatient hospitals in Nairobi in 2024

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, January 27, 2024

The National Hospital Insurance Fund (NHIF) is a health insurance provider in Kenya. It is a government state corporation with the mandate of providing accessible, affordable, sustainable and quality health insurance. Patients around the capital city can enjoy these services through the accredited outpatient NHIF hospitals in Nairobi.

In July 2015, the NHIF National Scheme launched outpatient services for all the members and their dependents. The NHIF outpatient services are offered at the client's preferred hospital. The clients are also at liberty to choose, change and confirm the already existing hospital every quarter.

Specialized services offered

NHIF outpatient hospitals in Nairobi offer comprehensive coverage of various pre-defined services. This comes in handy in helping the government to offer Universal Health Coverage to all its citizens. Some of the specialservices covered by the NHIF outpatient include:

  • Cancer package/oncology package: Includes coverage of chemotherapy, radiotherapy, and brachytherapy for cervix and prostate cancer for up to 10 sessions annually.

How to choose NHIF hospitals online

NHIF has upgraded services that allow their clients to access services through their application or NHIF web self-care. Below are steps to follow when choosing NHIF hospital online.

  • Visit the NHIF SelfCare online portal.
  • Input your national ID number.
  • You will receive a one-time password on your phone.
  • Enter the OTP and click to verify.
  • Click on the facilities tab on the homepage.
  • Select the county and the facility location.
  • Choose the NHIF hospital you prefer.
  • Repeat the process for each and every dependent.

    • What does NHIF cover in private hospitals?

    The coverage of the National Hospital Insurance Fund (NHIF) in private hospitals in Kenya may vary depending on the specific plan and package selected by the member. Generally, NHIF covers the cost of inpatient care, outpatient care, and some specialized services in private hospitals.

    Inpatient care includes the cost of hospitalization, accommodation, nursing care, laboratory tests, diagnostic tests, and surgery. Outpatient care covers consultations, laboratory tests, and diagnostic tests. Some specialized services that NHIF may cover in private hospitals include renal dialysis, cancer treatment, and specialized surgeries.
