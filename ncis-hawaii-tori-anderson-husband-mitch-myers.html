NCIS: Hawai'i star Tori Anderson's famous husband: Watch them co-star together

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 30, 2024

Tori Anderson's NCIS: Hawai'i character Kate Whistler is no doubt a fan favorite on the spin-off procedural show, partly due to her on-screen romance with Yasmine Al-Bustami's Lucy Tara.

While fans are deeply invested in her character's relationship, how much do you know about her love life off-screen? Find out all about her husband here. 

Who is Tori Anderson's husband? 

Tori Anderson is married to actor Mitch Myers – and the pair even appeared in a movie together!

In 2021, Kate starred in the romantic comedy, You May Kiss the Bridesmaid, as food influencer Scarlett Bailey, who lands a job as a pastry chef in one of NYC's top restaurants. Mitch played Scarlett's boyfriend, Geoff, in the movie. Watch them in a scene together in the clip below.

Posting about the movie on Instagram at the time, Mitch wrote: "During the early days of the pandemic we were fortunate enough to be cast in same movie - You May Kiss The Bridesmaid. And despite the monumental challenge of having to carry Tori's performance throughout the entire production, we did manage to have a few laughs together."

Mitch has few screen credits, having only appeared in three short films, as well as the rom-com. Little is known about his career away from acting. 

The couple first began dating in 2010 before tying the knot eight years later in a stunning ceremony in the Caribbean.

Mitch shared an incredible sunset photo from their big day to mark Tori's birthday in December 2018. In a tribute to his wife, he wrote in the comments section: "It's been a heck of a year, baby! From getting married in the Caribbean to a cross-continent move, I couldn't have asked for a better person to shake it up with! 

"I've been so fortunate to stand by your side for the past eight years and see you grow into the incredible, inspiring, and brilliant woman that you are today. I love you so much and I can't wait to see where our lives take us next."

Mitch clearly adores his wife and regularly shares sweet snaps of the couple on his Instagram page. In January, he marked their three-year anniversary with a gorgeous photo showing the pair dancing beside a willow tree in front of a lake. He wrote in the caption: "Happy Anniversary, baby... you know I'll dance with you anywhere."
