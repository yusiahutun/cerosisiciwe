Sultana Actress Fatima Says People Call Her Witch, Husband Snatcher: &quot;Mimi Ni Mama Kanisa&quot;

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 4, 2024
  • Actress Angie Magio popularly known as Fatima from Sultana TV show, has revealed people criticise her for her role on the screen
  • Fatima said she is often called a witch and homewrecker by people out there, including her churchmates
  • The multi-talented thespian said she doesn't choose the roles she play on shows but usually lands the wicked ones, hence why she's misjudged

Sultana actress Fatima, whose real name is Angie Magio, has revealed people confuse her acting role and the real her.

Sultana actress branded homewrecker

Fatima, who acts wicked roles in most of the shows she has featured in, while speaking about the challenges she faces out there during an interview with Citizen TV, said:

"Watu huniita mchawi. Wanasema natembea na bwana za watu na mimi navinja nyumba za watu (People call me a witch, husband snatcher and homewrecker)."

Fatima, who has been in the industry for years and has created a niche for herself, said people don't know how to differentiate Fatima's role from the real Angie.

"Mimi ni mama wa kanisa. (I'm a staunch Catholic, and some people in the church always criticise me, claiming I am a sinner. I try to explain to them Angie and Fatima are two different people, but still, they don't believe me," she said.

The veteran actress advised upcoming thespians never to give up and not to despise roles given in any show.

Fatima, who always keeps fans glued to the screen watching her do what she loves most on the popular Citizen TV show, said she was looking forward to becoming a movie producer.

Speaking in an interview with NTV, William said his wife is his biggest critic as well.

"Tunajua limits zetu na nikipata kazi mimi humwambia nimepata roles fulani na yeye ni my biggest critic (My wife and I know our limits. Whenever I get roles to play I always tell her, and she is my biggest critic)," he said.

The soft-spoken actor also said that he respects himself and there are some roles he can't play.

"Nikipewa script inasema nilale kitandani na mwanamke (If I'm given a script which says at some point I'll sleep in bed with a woman) I say no! I will not also kiss anyone on set. Those are a no for me," William said.
