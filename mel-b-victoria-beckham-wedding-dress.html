Victoria Beckham designs fellow Spice Girl Mel Bs wedding dress

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Finding the right wedding dress is a daunting task for any bride, but not if one of your oldest friends is a very successful fashion designer...

Spice Girl Melanie Brown – more commonly known as Mel B – turned to her friend and former bandmate Victoria Beckham, who has created the all-important dress for Brown's upcoming nuptials to hair stylist Rory McPhee.

“Victoria actually designed my dress and my mum’s dress,” Brown confirmed, during an appearance on US chat show Today with Hoda & Jenna. “It’s such a beautiful honour to get.”

Avalon//Getty Images

Like many modern brides, Brown isn't stopping at just one dress; she's planning on wearing three gowns in total (much like Sofia Richie Grainge did last year). “I don’t think it would be fair to have just one,” she said, laughing. “Just have as many as you want, at least three right?”

The singer also confirmed that she will be saying her vows in one of London’s most famous venues: St. Paul’s Cathedral – which is where Princess Diana and Prince Charles got married in 1981. The Spice Girl received an MBE in May 2022, for her services to charitable causes and vulnerable women, meaning that she's granted special access to get married in the same location as the royals.

Princess Diana Archive//Getty Images

The royal wedding in 1981 on the St. Paul’s Cathedral steps

The five Spice Girls have kept in touch since their band days, with Beckham saying in a previous interview with Harper's Bazaar that she "looks back and smiles" at that period of her life.

"I’m so proud of everything we achieved," Beckham said, "It was about making people embrace who they are, being happy with who they are, being the best version of themselves and for that to be celebrated. And the fact that it was OK to be a little bit different – why conform, you know? Now, I would like to think that I’m doing that through beauty and fashion."
