Congrats!The Bold and the Beautiful Star Jacqueline MacInnes Wood Is Engaged to Boyfriend Elan

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, October 4, 2024

Congratulations to The Bold and the Beautiful star Jacqueline MacInnes Wood (Steffy), who got engaged to boyfriend Elan Ruspoli over the weekend! “Biggest surprise of my life,” she declared on Instagram. “I’m engaged! Elan, you are my everything, and I can’t wait to begin this new chapter with you. I feel like the luckiest girl in the world. I love you beyond words. My soulmate.” She also used the hashtags “#RoadToRuspoli” and “#Jacquan4Eva.”

Jacqueline is best known for her role as Steffy Forrester on B&B, which she has been playing since May of 2008. She has earned two Daytime Emmy Award nominations for Outstanding Younger Actress in a Drama Series for her work in 2012 and 2013. Horror fans will recognize her as Olivia Castle, the poor young woman who endured a terrifying eye surgery in Final Destination 5. She has also appeared in the Lifetime movie, Her Husband’s Betrayal and hosted the E! television series Party On, where she traveled around the world to take viewers behind the party of the most exclusive party destinations.

Elan is an agent for Creative Artists Agency in Hollywood, and he and Jacqueline make an absolutely beautiful couple! We can’t wait to see the wedding, which should put even Steffy’s on-screen nuptials to shame!
