Cardi B shares NUDE imagery for new single Enough (Miami)... after being CENSORED by Instagram

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 7, 2024

The day after getting censored by Instagram, Cardi B defiantly Instastoried two all-nude alternative covers for her upcoming single Enough (Miami), which drops this Friday.

In it, the 31-year-old Grammy winner showcased her suspiciously curvaceous 5ft3in figure while reclining wearing nothing more than stripper platform heels and a massive wig cascading over the chaise lounge.

Cardi (born Belcalis Almánzar) ultimately went with a more close-up shot, which the Meta-owned social media platform promptly limited the reach of for 'non-followers.'

The Like What rapper - who boasts 168M Instagram followers - wrote: 'Hey guys, my cover is censored due to nudity, so if you could share it I'd appreciate it. Thank you so much for all the love!'
