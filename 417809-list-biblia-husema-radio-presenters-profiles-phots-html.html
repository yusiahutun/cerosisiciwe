List of Biblia Husema radio presenters, profiles, and photos

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, February 3, 2024

Biblia Husema Radio has risen through the ranks to become one of the leading Christian Radio stations in Kenya. The station has witnessed exponential growth over the years thanks to the hard work and dedication of Biblia Husema Radio presenters. It prides itself on having had a steady audience of more than one million listeners.

Christian radio has become a popular mode of broadcasting. Most Christian radio stations have a mission of encouraging their listeners in their faith through music and teaching. These broadcasters, including Biblia Husema, have taken their broadcasting a notch higher to deliver local news and current affairs.

About Biblia Husema

Biblia Husema Broadcasting, famously known as BHB, was started in 1953 by missionaries Bob and Lillian Davis of the Africa Inland Mission (AIM). In the beginning, the programmes consisted primarily of music and teachings. Afterwards, they featured interviews, devotions, hymn requests, and some history and biographies.

Since its inception, BHB has been committed to faithfully making God known as revealed in the Bible and through the life and ministry of Jesus Christ by the power of the Holy Spirit. It focuses on communicating the Good news of Salvation; and informing, instructing, and stimulating listeners and viewers.

Its vision is to transform Kenya, Africa, and the world through Christ-like centred broadcasting into true Disciples of Christ. Some of the most popular programmes aired on the channel include Hosanna Macheo, Mixed Flavours, Mavuno, and Jamie ya Mkristo.

List of Biblia Husema radio presenters

Biblia Husema FM presenters communicate the Good news of Salvation while at the same time informing, instructing, and stimulating the listener and viewers. Recently, the station conducted a mass firing, and only a few presenters were left. Here are the presenters who are currently playing host to the programmes aired on the channel.

1. Victor Mandala

Top on the list of Biblia Husema broadcasters is Victor Mandala. He joined the station in the capacity of a radio presenter and producer in 2007. Victor has been working for the station for more than 13 years. At the moment, he is the host of several Biblia Husema programmes, including Hosanna Macheo.

For his college education, he enrolled at the East Africa School of Media studies. While he is not at BHB studios, he works as an event organizer. He also works as the Master of Ceremony (MC) for concerts, funds drive, pre-weddings, weddings, and many other events.

2. Benson Mwanza

Benson is a minister of the gospel, journalist and radio producer, and presenter. He is the host of the Biblia Husema news known as BHB Shajara. Mwanza is also renowned for his role as the host of Mavuno Drive, Praise Thursday, and the Mixed Flavour shows. He was employed to work as the Swahili news presenter, sub-editor, and news reporter.

Later on, he was promoted to work as the news presenter for the station. He has worked for the station for more than 11 years. Benson graduated with a diploma in mass communication (radio production) from Kenya College of Communication Technology.

3. Captain Tjemo

The list of Biblia Husema presenters can never be complete without the mention of Captain Tjemo. He is a radio presenter and gospel artist. While he is not working at BHB, he sings and performs at gospel shows in different locations across the country.

He joined the station in September 2007, and he is still working there to date. This means that he has worked with BHB for more than 11 years. Before joining BHB, he worked as the news reporter for Sayare TV from September 2005 to March 2006. He is an EATI alumnus.

4. Casphine Oresi

Casphine is a journalist, news reporter, and voice-over artist. She is one of the presenters at BHB. She is the host of the Mavuno Drive show, which airs on the station from 4 PM to 6 PM on weekdays. She also hosts the Harvest Drive program. Oresi studied at Sunrise High School before enrolling at Zetech University.

There you have it. A comprehensive list of Biblia Husema radio presenters. These presenters play a critical role in fulfilling the vision of BHB, which is to spread the word of God as revealed through the Bible and the ministry of Jesus Christ. You can tune in to BHB to catch up with the presenters in action.
