Here Are the Lyrics to Luke Combs' 'Beer Never Broke My Heart'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, May 2, 2024

Luke Combs' "Beer Never Broke My Heart" lyrics are so catchy and witty, it's no surprise the rising superstar decided to name his 2019 tour after the song.

Combs is letting "Beer Never Broke My Heart" loose as a single finally, after performing it acoustically at concerts. Though fans didn't know the lyrics early on, they're learning quickly and it has become a fan-favorite — a hit before it's even officially been released to radio.

Combs' new song is an homage to beer — something that can never break his heart. He sings about everything else in life that can hurt a person, but ultimately comes to the conclusion that beer is the only safe bet!

"Beer Never Broke My Heart" follows his previous single, "Beautiful Crazy," which topped all five Billboard country charts for multiple weeks, something no other debut artist has ever achieved.

Who's ready to grab an ice cold long neck after listening to this song?
