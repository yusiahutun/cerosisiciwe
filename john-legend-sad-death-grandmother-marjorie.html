John Legend mourns death of his 'twin' grandmother just days after her birthday

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, April 4, 2024

The star paid tribute to Marjorie on Instagram

    John Legend is mourning the death of his grandmother Marjorie, who passed away on Tuesday morning just days after her 91st birthday.

"My beautiful grandmother, Marjorie Maxine Stephens, left this world early this morning, just a few days after her 91st birthday. We are so grateful for the life she lived, for the love she shared with her many children, grandchildren, great grandchildren, and beyond," he began.
