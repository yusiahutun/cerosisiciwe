Charissa Thompson STUNS Richard Sherman with hilarious comment on live TV... as ex-NFL star appears

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, February 10, 2024

Charissa Thompson pulled out a famous movie quote that stunned former NFL-cornerback-turned-broadcaster Richard Sherman on Thursday Night Football. 

'I've got nipples, can you milk me Greg?' Thompson asked, with Richard Sherman looking befuddled at the other end of the panel.  

Broadcaster Greg Olsen wasn't doing a fill-in for Amazon Prime. It was a callback to the 2000 film 'Meet the Parents' and Robert De Niro's utterance to Ben Stiller during an awkward family dinner. 


Sherman either didn't recognize the quote or was shocked to hear Thompson say it based on his reaction. 

Thompson pulling out the quote wasn't out of thin air. The panelists were exchanging Christmas gifts with Thompson receiving a farmer's almanac and a dairy pale.

The quote is a memorable one for movie fans. 

It was prestigious enough to be nominated for the American Film Institute's '100 Years ... 100 Movie Quotes' in 2005.

Amazon Prime hasn't commented on the incident as it made it onto the broadcast unedited. 

It's unclear how quickly someone explained the movie quote to Sherman. 

Thompson and Sherman are typically joined by Tony Gonzalez, Andrew Whitworth, and Ryan Fitzpatrick for Thursday Night Football.
