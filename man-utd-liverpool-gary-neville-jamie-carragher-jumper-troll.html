Carragher trolls Gary Neville &amp; Man Utd with Christmas jumper ahead of Liverpool tie as fans ask

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, February 20, 2024

JAMIE CARRAGHER has hilariously trolled Gary Neville with a cheeky Christmas jumper ahead of Manchester United’s trip to Liverpool on Sunday.

The Red Devils visit Anfield for the first time since March’s 7-0 Premier League thrashing.

Stars like Mo Salah and Darwin Nunez ran riot on a miserable day for Erik ten Hag’s side.

And many are predicting a similar scoreline this weekend, with Liverpool top of the table and United coming off the back of a 3-0 home defeat to Bournemouth.

The Red Devils were also dumped out of Europe this week as they lost 1-0 to Bayern Munich.

But Carragher compounded United and pal Neville’s misery by dusting off his best Christmas jumper.

The Anfield legend wore a knitted top showing him smiling in front of a dour Neville with “7-Nil” in the background.

The festive jumper was completed with Carragher saying in a speech bubble: “Oh I wish I could relive this everyday…

“When the Kop starts singing and the Reds being to play…”

Neville wasn’t too impressed with the cheeky dig as he later joined Carragher on Sky Sports.
