Beneath Us, Actress Lynn Collins is Happily Married! See Her Husband and Family

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, January 1, 0001

Lynn Collins was married to Matthew Boyle. The couple is blessed with a son, Asher Hendrix Boyle.

American actress Lynn Collins is working on the horror thriller Beneath Us alongside Rigo Sanchez and Josue Aguirre. The movie is all set to be released in 2018. She is also renowned for performing in movies like X-Men Origins: Wolverine and John Carter.

For her fans, Collins' professional life is an open book, but what about her personal life? After her divorce from her first husband, Steven Strait, Collins is living happily with Matthew Boyle. Let's have a closer exploration of Collins's married life.  

Lynn Collins's Married Life With Husband Matthew Boyle     

Collins is currently married to Matthew Boyle. The couple started dating in 2014, and after a month, the duo got engaged in November 2013. 

They tied the knot in November 2014 after ten months of engagement. They together welcomed a son, Asher Hendrix Boyle, in January 2015.

Lynn Collins's Previous Relationship

Before Boyle, Collins married an actor, model, and rock singer Steven Strait. The former couple started dating in 2003 and eventually tied the knot on December 23, 2007.

Their relationship lasted for about six years, and they separated in 2013. Their divorce was settled in March 2013.

Collins soon moved on and started an affair with the Canadian actor Keanu Reeves in the same year. Their relationship was short-lived, as they got apart three months later.
