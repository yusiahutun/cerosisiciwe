She's lauded for her incredible figure, and Brooke Burke-Charvet is the first to admit she's had a little help with it.

The presenter, now 40, had breast augmentation in her 20s and a later adjustment, and she's now speaking up in favour of the procedure for Mentor, a medical supple company that makes implants.

Fine form: Brooke Burke has filmed an segment for a company that make breast implants, she showed off her great figure in Malibu today 

The mother-of-four is starring in a series of five videos for Mentor, the huge medical supply company that makes breast implants, according to RadarOnline.com.

In a promotional campaign called Love Your Look, Brooke discusses all the reasons a woman might want to change the look of her breasts.

'If there is one thing we know about our breasts, it's that they do their own thing,' she explained.

'If there’s one thing we all know, it’s that our curves belong to us,' Brooke added, while never addressing her own surgery.

Busy mom: Brooke, who has admitted to breast augmentation in the past, was shopping for her family

Honest: Brooke has always been open about her surgery

'And we have every right to love the way they look. So when it comes to cosmetic surgery, if there’s something you don’t like about your body and a cosmetic alteration will help, then why not?

Brooke, whose first husband was plastic surgeon Garth Fisher, did tell ET about her decision to go under the knife, and insisted: 'I've always been open in the things I've done in my life.'

Impressive: Brooke tweeted this picture of her playing volleyball with her family a couple of days ago

Handsome pair: Brooke throws to her husband, David

The sexy brunette is now married to former Baywatch star, David Charvet.

But her figure isn't all down to surgery, as the television star is also a gym fanatic.

The busy mother has her own range of fitness DVDs, runs a blog and has been the cover star of exercise magazines.

She once told OK! magazine she works out for at least 45 minutes a minimum of three times per week.
