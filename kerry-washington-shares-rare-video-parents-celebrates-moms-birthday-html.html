Kerry Washington shares a rare video of both her parents as she celebrates her mom's birthday

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Kerry Washington took to Instagram on Wednesday to celebrate her mom Valerie Washington's birthday with a sweet video.

The Scandal star, 45 - who recently celebrated Christmas eve with a medley of holiday songs - shared a rare clip of her with both Valerie and her father Earl Washington, writing, 'Cheers to you, Mom. Happy Birthday!!!!!!!!!! I love you!!!!!'

She also took to her Stories to champion her mom's favorite cause and ask fans to donate to the Bronx Children's museum for her special day.

'So do you have any resolutions or goals going into this new year of your life?' the star asked her mom.

'I do, I do. But first I need my glasses,' Valerie replied, handing a confused Kerry a pair of her reading glasses.

'Okay, why do I need them?' Kerry asked.

'What?' Valerie replied, suddenly handing her daughter a glass of wine, while holding her own glass in the other hand.

'Oh, wine glasses,' Kerry replied with a smile, finally getting the joke.

Jokester: 'I do, I do. But first I need my glasses,' Valerie replied, before pulling out two wine glasses

The mother and daughter duo then clinked their glasses for a toast, while her dad could be heard saying, 'Wait a minute guys, that sounds like a bad dad joke.'

'Or a good mom joke,' Kerry responded, as the trio shared a laugh.

Kerry then took to her Stories with a stunning photo of her and Valerie in matching orange gowns.

Dear to her heart: She also took to her Stories to champion her mom's favorite cause and ask fans to donate to the Bronx Children's museum for her special day

In the caption, she championed a cause near to her mother's heart: 'Today is my beautiful, smart, graceful, and generous mother's birthday. She's dedicated her life to serving children from The bronx and has worked so closely with @bronxchildrensmuseum to support their opening.'

'Your donation DIRECTLY IMPACTS the lives of children and families; provides FREE ADMISSION FOR ALL during the Museum's opening period; and supports ONGOING OUTREACH PROGRAMMING throughout The Bronx. Any donation would mean the world to us.' 

Washington has been spending plenty of time with family during the holidays, and was seen making a rare public appearance with her husband of nine years - The Good Nurse actor Nnamdi Asomugha - to Kenny Leon's Broadway revival of Topdog/Underdog at the John Golden Theatre in Manhattan last Tuesday.

Play date! Washington made a rare public appearance with her husband of nine years - The Good Nurse actor Nnamdi Asomugha (L) - to Kenny Leon's Broadway revival of Topdog/Underdog at the John Golden Theatre in Manhattan last Tuesday

The Reasonable Doubt producer-star and the 41-year-old Emmy-nominated producer are proud parents of eight-year-old daughter Isabelle Amarachi and six-year-old son Caleb Kelechi.

Kerry will next play relationship therapist and single mom Paige Alexander opposite Delroy Lindo as her ex-con father Edwin Alexander in Tracy McMillan's semi-autobiographical eight-episode comedy Unprisoned, which premieres next year on Hulu.

Washington and the 70-year-old Tony nominee also serve as executive producers on the project headed up by showrunner Yvette Lee Bowser, which also features actors Faly Rakotohavana and Jordyn McIntosh.
