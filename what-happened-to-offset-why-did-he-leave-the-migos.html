What Happened To Offset- Why Did He Leave The Migos? Arrest Charges Incident Might Be The Cause

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 12, 2024

Many people are speculating if Cardi B's husband, Rapper Offset, has quit the group after he stopped following Quavo and Takeoff on Instagram.

Offset is an American rapper who has recorded music as a solo artist as well as as a member of Migos, a rap group that includes his cousin Quavo and Quavo's nephew Takeoff.

Fans of Migos are worried and dismayed after speculations that the hip-hop band is breaking up surfaced.

Fans are shocked because the three rappers were supposed to play at Toronto's Rolling Loud Festival in September.

What Happened To Rapper Offset- Why Did He Leave The Migos?

Migos' fans believe the group is no longer a trio. On Instagram, Offset has unfollowed his bandmates Quavo and Takeoff. There have been beef allegations among the group's members.

Offset's wife, Cardi B, is alleged to have unfollowed the two members as well. As word of the rumors spread on social media, Twitter users expressed their fury and disgust.

[image-1]

"Migos spliting up would be worse than the Beatles," one fan wrote, comparing the Migos' breakup to that of one of the biggest bands of all time.

According to DJ Akademiks, who claims he spoke with Offset regarding the group's future, the alleged split between Migos is unlikely to happen.

Ak said in a recent interview with TMZ that he recently spoke with Offset regarding the suspected Migos split.

Rapper Offest Arrest Charges And Incident Causing Fallouts 

Officers with the Beverly Hills Police Department nabbed Migos rapper Offset, whose real name is Kiari Cephus, during a routine traffic stop.

Authorities received a report of someone pointing a pistol from inside a vehicle, according to police. The caller gave police a license plate number, and the car was immediately halted.

Offset, who is married to artist Cardi B, live-streamed part of his encounter with cops on Instagram Live as it happened. What transpired before and after the video was posted is unknown.

Before the video concludes, Offset exits the vehicle and inquires as to why the officers are grasping his wrist.

According to a BHPD statement released on Twitter, a 20-year-old passenger was arrested and charged with carrying a concealed weapon and a loaded pistol in public. According to Beverly Hills police, Offset, who was also in the car, was held but not arrested or prosecuted.

How Is Rapper Offset Related To Quavo And TakeOff? 

Some fans are concerned that if Offset does indeed leave Migos, there will be a split in the family. For the uninitiated, Migos' three members are all connected to one another.

Quavo is Takeoff's uncle, whereas Offset is Quavo's cousin. In Atlanta, the trio grew up together.

Migos was created in 2008. The band has received three BET Awards and has been nominated for a number of other awards.

[image-2]

Migos rose to prominence after the release of their track "Versace" in 2013. Yung Rich Nation, the trio's debut studio album, was released in 2015. Their tune "Bad and Boujee" became an Internet sensation in 2017, producing a slew of memes around the lyrics "raindrop, drop top," which Offset performed.

Many critics believe Offset commanded the song, which reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100 ranking in the United States. Culture, the trio's second studio album, debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart in the United States in 2017.
