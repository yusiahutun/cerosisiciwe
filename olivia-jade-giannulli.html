Olivia Jade Giannulli News - Us Weekly

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

Name:Olivia Jade Giannulli

Relationship:Single

Birthday:September 28, 1999

About

Olivia Jade Giannulli was born on September 28, 1999, to actress Lori Loughlin and fashion designer Mossimo Giannulli. She rose to fame as a YouTuber while attending Marymount High School in Los Angeles and developed a following of nearly 2 million as of April 2019. Olivia became a successful influencer after launching her channel in August 2014, signing deals with Amazon, Sephora, TreSemme and Princess Polly. In 2018, Olivia tried to trademark Olivia Jade Beauty and Olivia Jade, but her application was rejected due to poor punctuation. Olivia’s trademark application was later refiled in April 2019 and is pending review.

The beauty influencer’s YouTube videos follow her friendships, relationships, fame, fashion advice and makeup tutorials. However, in 2017, Olivia came under fire for crashing her car while filming herself singing Stevie Wonder’s song “Signed, Sealed, Delivered I’m Yours” on her cell phone for a video, promoting criticism from her followers.

Olivia faced backlash again in March 2019 after her parents were arrested for allegedly paying $500,000 to secure her and her older sister, Isabella Rose Giannulli, admission into the University of Southern California as designated crew team recruits, although neither girl has played the sport. Us Weekly confirmed in October 2019 that Olivia and Bella were no longer enrolled at the university. Amid the scandal, Olivia had been staying at Jackson Guthy’s home in Malibu. They dated on and off between January 2019 and August 2021.

Olivia joined the cast of Dancing With the Stars season 30, performing with Val Chmerkovskiy until their elimination in November 2021.

Us confirmed in December 2021 that Olivia was casually seeing Euphoria star Jacob Elordi. The pair went their separate ways by August 2022.
