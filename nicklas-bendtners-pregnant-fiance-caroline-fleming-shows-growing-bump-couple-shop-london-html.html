Nicklas Bendtner's pregnant fiance Caroline Fleming shows off her growing bump as the couple shop

Updated: 13:09 EST, 5 August 2010

She only announced her pregnancy three weeks ago but Caroline Fleming's bump is already burgeoning nicely.

The fianceé of Arsenal star Nicklas Bendtner looking radiant as she and the footballer went shopping in London this week.

She couldn't keep the radiant smile off her face as she and the striker made their way back his car.

Parents to be: Nicklas Bendtner and his pregnant fianceé Caroline Fleming stroll through London after a shopping trip

It is the first time that Caroline has publicly showed off her bump to the cameras, but going on her beaming smile, she didn't seem to mind.

Caroline, who, at 35, is 13 years Bendtner's senior, looked as though the extra baby weight was also starting to take its toll.

In a pair of trendy flats, she clutched her blacks Louboutin heels which had possibly got too uncomfortable for her to walk on.

The couple announced they were expecting their first child last month with Bendtner, 22, making the announcement on his personal website.

Early days: Nicklas and Caroline, who had just found out she was pregnant, attend a wedding in Copenhagen in May

The couple have been dating for almost a year after they met on a reality show.

Nicklas, who earns £2.5million a year at Arsenal, was a guest on the show and the pair did a photoshoot to promote his appearance.

Caroline said of him at the time: 'He is charming and bright and he's very willing to help other people.

'You never know when our paths will cross again.'

Caroline is a member of the Danish Royal family and has her own castle, which the Baroness renovated for the reality TV show on which she met Bendtner.

He played for his native Denmark in the World Cup this summer but has ruled himself out of starting the impending Premiership season due to injury.
