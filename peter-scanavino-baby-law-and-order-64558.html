Congrats!Law &amp; Order: SVU Star Peter Scanavino and Wife Expecting Baby No. 2

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 23, 2024

There’s another Law & Order: SVU baby on the way!

Following the announcements that his costars Kelli Giddish and Ice T are both expecting new additions, Peter Scanavino, 35, revealed he and his wife, Lisha Bai, are also pregnant with their second child.

Yes, @PeterScanavino @FINALLEVEL @TheKelliGiddish ALL infanticipating. #SVUMostFertileDramaThereIs

— Warren Leight (@warrenleightTV) July 28, 2015

“Breaking: You won’t believe this but @PeterScanavino just checked in. He and his lovely wife Lisha expecting their 2nd in January! #SVUNursery,” the show’s executive producer Warren Leight tweeted on Tuesday, July 28.

Leight then published another message on the micro-blogging site confirming Peter, Kelli and Ice T are in fact all expecting babies!

Peter, Kelli and Ice T on Law & Order: SVU in January 2015.

“Yes, @PeterScanavino @FINALLEVEL @TheKelliGiddish ALL infanticipating. #SVUMostFertileDramaThereIs,” he wrote.

Congratulations to Peter and his wife, Lisha, on their exciting news!
