Meet the Experts

  • Robert Finney is a board-certified dermatologist at Entière Dermatology in New York City.
  • Purvisha Patel is a board-certified dermatologist at Advanced Dermatology in Memphis, Tennessee, and the founder of Visha Skincare.

Why is vitamin C beneficial in skincare?

Vitamin C is primarily known for its skin brightening qualities. In addition, it's "A potent antioxidant that prevents free radicals from breaking down collagen and elastin," Patel explains. "It can also help decrease pigment production."

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

When is the best time to apply a vitamin C serum?

According to experts, the best time to apply a vitamin C serum is in the morning on clean, dry skin before using at least an SPF 30. Patel adds, "Vitamin C helps to compound the protection of your SPF.”

Which products should you avoid when using a vitamin C serum?

As some vitamin C serums can be extra potent in your routine, it's not surprising that it doesn't always play well with other ingredients. "Using too many products with exfoliating agents, such as AHAs, retinol, and vitamin C all at once can make the skin peel and lead to sensitivity. Using only one active ingredient at a time helps minimize this," Patel explains. If you're concerned about using products with these ingredients, try using them at night, and vitamin C in the morning.
