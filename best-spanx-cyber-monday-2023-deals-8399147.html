Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 12, 2024

You know when you find, try, see, or eat something really good, and then it’s over in the blink of an eye? Black Friday can sometimes feel like that. Really good deals are gone 24 hours later, but thankfully, Cyber Monday sales are here to save the day, giving those who might experience #FOMO or those who still need to stock up on other things another chance to snag great deals. And right now, the best ones can be found at Spanx — so if you missed the brand’s Black Friday sale, know that its Cyber Monday markdowns are just as epic.

Spanx’s Black Friday sale is iconic. It’s the one day in the entire year the brand puts everything on sale for 20 percent off — including already marked-down pieces — which means savings are as high as 70 percent. But Spanx’s Cyber Monday sale? We love it just as much because we can still snag major deals on the brand's most popular styles, including leather leggings, bodysuits, the buttery soft AirEssentials line, and more.

Now, as a Spanx-obsessed fashion editor, I like to say that I am somewhat of an expert on the brand. My family, friends, and coworkers ask me all the time what’s worth getting. TBH, I think everything is worth trying, but if you said I could only buy five things, below is everything I would choose in the final hours of Spanx’s once-a-year Cyber Monday sale. If something catches your eye, don't wait to shop; Cyber Monday ends tonight, and these fleeting discounts will go with it.

The Perfect Pant, Wide Leg

When I say these are the only wide-leg pants you need, I mean it. I’ve waxed poetic about them before, got my mom and sister obsessed, and have, to this day, amassed an impressive collection of the style in various colors. They look like the most luxe designer trousers but feel like the most comfy sweats. People will compliment you nonstop on them, but little do they know you're basically wearing loungewear. They’re now marked down to $134, and before you ask, yes, I’m buying another pair. 

Low-Maintenance Silk Button-Down Shirt

Silk can be tricky but it looks oh-so-good. It’s a constant struggle I dealt with before discovering Spanx’s Low-Maintenance Silk Button-Down Shirt that’s true to its name. The shirt feels and looks so luxe — it’s durable silk that’s soft and flowy but doesn’t feel overly delicate. Plus, it can actually be thrown in the washing machine without worry. No longer do you need to hit up the dry cleaner for these delicates — and TBH, that peace of mind (and savings) is easily worth the shirt’s now-$158 price tag. 

Leather-Like Flare Pants

Cool leather pants that look like real leather but aren’t as tricky as real leather? Count me in. These Leather-Like Flare Pants are the latest example that Spanx is changing the game when it comes to wearing, well, leather. They look real, but the material actually moves and stretches like regular ol’ fabric — as in its flexible, so you won’t feel like you’re constricted. Bonus: There’s also built-in tummy control, and you all know I love a secret, flattering hack.

Booty Boost Active 7/8 Leggings

There are regular leggings and then there are Spanx leggings. The latter are superior to all, and if you haven’t yet experienced the magic that is the brand's famous, game-changing, celeb- and editor-worn Booty Boost leggings, now is the time. They’re 20 percent off, but that’s not the best part: The butt-lifting magic is — in fact, it’s the ultimate selling point of these bottoms that Jennifer Garner wears to death. My booty looks incredible whenever I wear them, plus, they're comfy and all-around flattering, so whenever I go somewhere that calls for leggings, you’ll catch me in these. No other leggings compare, and for $78, I think I might buy a few fresh pairs. 

Tummy-Shaping Tights

Tights are a fall and winter must, but finding good tights isn’t easy, unless you heed my advice and snag this tummy-shaping pair from Spanx while it’s just $19. Most drugstore tights are fine; they do the job, but they probably only last one wear. Spanx’s tights? You’ll have them for much longer thanks to their long-lasting durability. Plus, they’re secretly flattering, too, with built-in tummy control and thigh-shaping technology that takes classic tights to another level.
