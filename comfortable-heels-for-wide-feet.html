Best Extended-Width Heels for Women

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

Chelsea Paris Ace Mule

Chelsea Paris Ace Mule

The Ace Mule by Chelsea Paris is the perfect way to add height while choosing comfort first with a sculptural thick heel. The buttery soft leather will stretch to add more width. 

Margaux NY The City Sandal

Margaux NY The City Sandal

The City Sandal is the absolute best warm-weather silhouette to get you back into the streets in style. Extended sizing ranges in wide widths from 4 through a size 14. This is a timeless shape that adds polish to any ensemble. 

Bells & Becks The Luna Black

Bells & Becks The Luna Black

Now 25% Off

This modern update of the slingback features a padded sole, a stretchy elastic band to secure your feet, and a wide-width footbed sizing up to a 12.

Staud Frankie Ruched Slide Sandal

Staud Frankie Ruched Slide Sandal

The ruched effect of this sandal presents an elegant modern touch and will also stretch for comfort and extra room. 

Inez Sofia in Emerald Suede

Inez Sofia in Emerald Suede

The shoe experts at Inez from Spain designed the most comfortable barely-there sandal. You can customize your width size with the roomy option when checking out. 

Ferragamo Vara Bow Pump Shoes

Ferragamo Vara Bow Pump Shoes

The Vara Bow Pump block heels by Ferragamo should be in every woman's wardrobe. A timeless silhouette meant for many occasions, they are available in wide-width sizing.

Stuart Weitzman The Anny 70 Pump

Stuart Weitzman The Anny 70 Pump

The perfect LBP (little black pump) in suede by Stuart Weitzman will be worn many times over.

Nomasei Nono Loafer in Pompeii

Nomasei Nono Loafer in Pompeii

The cool-girl loafer by Nomasei features a delicate square-toe shape that is ideal for a bit of extra room. 

Good American The Standout Square-Toe Slide Sandals

Good American The Standout Square-Toe Slide Sandals

These suede square-toe mules by Good American go up to a size 11W and have a comfort cushioned insole and gold accents.

Marion Parke Iris Leather Braided Sandal with Block Heel

Marion Parke Iris Leather Braided Sandal with Block Heel

Certified podiatrist and designer Marion Parke knows how to craft the most comfortable shoes in the market. This braided block heel is elegant and versatile.
