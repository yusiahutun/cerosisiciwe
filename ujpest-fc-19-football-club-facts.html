Újpest FC has a rich history

Újpest FC, also known as Újpesti TE, is one of the oldest and most successful football clubs in Hungary. It was founded in 1885 and has been a prominent figure in Hungarian football ever since. With a history spanning over 135 years, Újpest FC has built a legacy that is deeply intertwined with Hungarian football culture.
