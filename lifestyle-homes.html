The best dried flowers for a spring style refresh

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, March 2, 2024

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Dried flowers: summer arrangement

Your London Florist 'Last Summer Haze' Dried Bouquet

Dried flowers: summer arrangement

Your London Florist 'Last Summer Haze' Dried Bouquet

This pretty bouquet is part of Your London Florist’s collaboration with Selfridges and, despite their name, they deliver nationwide, so you can enjoy them whenever you’re based. A gorgeous mix of sunset tones and leafy greens, this bunch includes dried poppies, helichrysum, glixia and eucalyptus, so that you can evoke those late summer days all year round.

Dried flowers: Pampas grass arrangement

'Mayfair' Pampas Grass Vase Set

Dried flowers: Pampas grass arrangement

'Mayfair' Pampas Grass Vase Set

Fashion’s go-to luxury florist, Flowerbx specialises in single-variety bouquets (and has provided blooms for Burberry, Tom Ford and Michael Kors, among others). If you’re after elevated neutrals, our pick of the dried options is this classic natural pampas, which will make a dramatic statement in any interiors scheme. Choose from 20 or 40 tall stems, which you can buy with or without the vase.

Dried flowers: Pink arrangement

'Pink Cockatil' Bouquet

Dried flowers: Pink arrangement

'Pink Cockatil' Bouquet

Haute Florist, another of our favourite luxury flower delivery services, has some stunning bouquets on offer. If a bright and feminine aesthetic appeals, this dried bunch is for you. Pink bunny tails add some fun to an otherwise classic pampas bouquet, making for a whimsical and cheery bunch to welcome in the new season. There’s also the option to add one of three vases, some chocolates or a bottle of champagne to your order, should you need.

Dried flowers: green arrangement

Large 'Matcha' Bouquet

Dried flowers: green arrangement

Large 'Matcha' Bouquet

Designed with a contemporary, naturally wild aesthetic in mind, Shida Florist's selection of dried bunches is really impressive – you can even design your own. Our favourite is the Matcha collection, which has an elegant, fresh feel. Lush greenery and delicate flowers bring the outside in, and perfectly pair with any colour scheme.

Dried flowers: Minimalist arrangement

'The Wilma' Dried Bouquet

Dried flowers: Minimalist arrangement

'The Wilma' Dried Bouquet

We love this rustic-style bouquet from the original letterbox flower company, Bloom and Wild. The mix of beige palm spears and bleached pampas grass makes a great pared-back option for minimalist spaces – and it's an excellent value option to boot.

Dried flowers: autumnal arrangement

'Salted Caramel' Dried Flower Bouquet

Dried flowers: autumnal arrangement

'Salted Caramel' Dried Flower Bouquet

An eye-catching arrangement of earthy yellow, orange and chocolate-hued flowers, this dried bunch is artfully undone and oh-so chic. Containing palm leaves, poppy heads, bunny tails and preserved beech leaves, it's a great year-round arrangement. What's more, Botanique Workshop offer next-day delivery, making this the perfect choice for a last-minute gift.

Dried flowers: lavender arrangement

Dried Lavender Flower Bunch

Dried flowers: lavender arrangement

Dried Lavender Flower Bunch

Simple, classic and guaranteed to please, dried lavender not only looks beautiful, but will scent your home wonderfully, too. This well-priced bunch from Flowerfix has excellent reviews, and would look rustic and charming arranged in a plain white vase on a bedside table, or hung upside down in a kitchen for a dash of traditional style.

Dried flowers: modern arrangement

'Wildflower Bloom' Bouquet

Dried flowers: modern arrangement

'Wildflower Bloom' Bouquet

The plush, almost velvet-like textures of this dried bouquet make it feel elevated and luxurious. With dramatic pops of navy and pink buried among fluffy natural grasses, these whimsical wildflowers look stunning styled in a simple stone or glass vase. This bouquet contains eucalyptus and lavender so – as an added bonus – it'll smell gorgeous, too.

Dried flowers: eccentric arrangement

'Everlasting Love' Bouquet

Dried flowers: eccentric arrangement

'Everlasting Love' Bouquet

London-based florist Grace & Thorn specialises in striking, off-beat styles, and this flowing bouquet is a case in point. As G&T’s arrangements are seasonal, the flowers may vary, but during spring you can expect to see a mix of delphinium, yarrow, palak, statice, roses, long stemmed pampas and limonium grass. Arriving in a chic glass vase, it makes a beautiful gift.

Dried flowers: rose arrangement

'Elodie' Preserved Rose Bouquet

Dried flowers: rose arrangement

'Elodie' Preserved Rose Bouquet

Preserving delicate roses is no easy feat, but Dried – an independent Edinburgh boutique, which makes its bunches to order and by hand – manages it. This stunning bouquet contains three, pale pink preserved roses, alongside dried gypsophila, globe thistles, pretty ruscus and preserved eucalyptus.
