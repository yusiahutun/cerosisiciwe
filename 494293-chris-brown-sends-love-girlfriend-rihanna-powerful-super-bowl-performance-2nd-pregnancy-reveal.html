Chris Brown Sends Love to Ex-Girlfriend Rihanna After Powerful Super Bowl Performance, 2nd Pregnancy

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 18, 2024
  • Chris Brown shocked the world when he took to his social media pages to seemingly react to his ex-girlfriend Rihanna's Super Bowl 2023 performance
  • The Take A Bow singer also took the world by surprise when she announced that she was expecting her second baby with rapper A$AP Rocky months after welcoming a baby boy
  • Taking to his Instagram stories, Breezy showed support for his former lover alongside some emojis

Chris Brown has shown his ex-girl Rihanna some love after her flopped performance during the recent Super Bowl game.

Not only did Rihanna walk her fans down memory lane with a medley of some of her greatest hits, but the Unfaithful hitmaker also revealed that she was pregnant with her second baby almost nine months after welcoming a baby boy with boyfriend A$AP Rocky.

Before her Super Bowl performance, the Fenty boss opened up about her return to the stage after taking a seven-year break from performing live on stage.
