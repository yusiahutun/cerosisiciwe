20 funniest country names: Which continent features the most?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 1, 2024

Which are the funniest country names you have heard of? To which continent do these countries belong? Different countries worldwide have names that depend on the history of the founding fathers. Some are interesting, while others seem hilarious.

  • Funniest country names

The history of groups of people living in a particular region can explain more about the origin of the name of their particular country. However, from the pronunciation, some names sound a bit funny to most people.

Funniest country names

A funny country name goes a long way in creating a lasting impression on the nation’s visitors. According to AnswersAfrica, here is the list of the funniest country names.

1. Djibouti - Africa

Located on the horn of Africa, Djibouti is a small country between Somalia and Ethiopia. It has roughly 1.1 million people and a large population of Arabic speakers.

With modern slang in play, the term Djibouti is humorous and can be subject to teases. It is one of the funniest country names in the world.

2. Hungary - Europe

When you think about it, Hungary sounds like a short form of "who’s hungry". It is located in the Carpathian basin. It is a sovereign state in Europe.

Hungary got its independence from the Soviet Union on October 23rd, 1989. The capital of Hungary is Budapest- one of the most beautiful cities in the world.

3. Turkey - Middle East

Whenever the name Turkey comes up in a conversation, you may need to clarify in some context that you are talking about the country, not the bird. This has to be one of the funniest country names in the middle east.

Officially the Republic of Türkiye lies between the Anatolian peninsula in western Asia and the Balkan peninsula in southeast Europe.

4. Niger - Africa

It is located in West Africa and borders Burkina Faso, Mali, Libya, Chad, Benin and Algeria. It is a land-locked country. The capital of Niger is Niamey, and the country has some of the largest Uranium deposits in the world.

5. Papua New Guinea - Oceania

If you are looking for a country full of biodiversity, then Papua New Guinea is the destination for you. The population in this beautiful country is diverse, and it has been a popular tourist destination. The capital of Papua New Guinea is Port Moresby.

6. Russia – Europe

Russia is the largest country in the world. Different people pronounce the name differently, and it comes out funny. It has to be one of the funny country names in Europe and Asia.

7. Turkmenistan - Asia

The ‘Stan’ countries have a play of words that will mess up your tongue while trying to pronounce them. Turkmenistan sounds like a distant cousin of Turkey. This country is located in the central part of Asia.

Turkmenistan is on the list of the funny Asian country names of all time. Unfortunately, it is difficult to pronounce and also long to write.

8. Liechtenstein - Europe

The country has a long and tricky name to pronounce. It is mostly German-speaking. In medieval history, Liechtenstein had several castles, and the beauty of her alpine sceneries is unmatched.

9. Montenegro - Europe

Montenegro makes it to the list of funny European country names. It is a small country in Europe. It has a population of about seven hundred thousand people. The dominant languages are Montenegrin and Serbian.

The name sounds a little offensive based on historical happenings. However, it has become one of the most popular tourist destinations for its beautiful mountains and coastlines.

10. Greece - Europe

Greece is located in Southeastern Europe. It has a vast number of small islands spread across it. More often than not, the history of Greece has pointed out why the country could be the cradle of western civilisation.

11. Oman - Asia

If you are a citizen of Oman, it would be weird for you to exclaim using, "oh man!" Though this country’s name is not English, it sounds like English phrases.

This could bring about humorous confusion. The Sultanate of Oman has its Capital in Muscat. The country is well known for its ancient irrigation systems that have gained popularity over the centuries.

12. Nauru - Oceania

Located in the Pacific, Nauru is the third smallest country behind Vatican city and Tuvalu. Although it’s a small island, Nauru is not a popular tourist destination like many other islands.

Uniquely, Nauru doesn’t have a capital within its geographical location. Instead, its parliament is based in Yaren.

13. Palau - Oceania

Palau is an archipelago of the many islands in Oceania. It is located in the Pacific. It has collectively around 500 islands.

Palau gained her independence in 1994. The beautiful vegetation and sandy beaches have become the leading tourist attractions for this region in the Pacific. The Capital of Palau is Ngerulmud.

14. Christmas Island - Asia

It is not clear why or how the name Christmas Island came to place. However, the name has a unique sense of humour. Christmas Island is one of the unique and funny country names in Asia.

15. Iceland - Europe

The funniest bit about this country’s name is that the land is super green. The irony in the name spreads a little cheer when you think about it. Only about 11% of Iceland is covered with ice.

The rest Is a collection of beautiful landscapes with hot springs all around. From September to March, Iceland is one of the top tourist destination countries as the northern lights can be seen. The capital of Iceland is Reykjavik.

16. Chad - Africa

Chad is another funniest country in Africa. To add to this, the capital of Chad is N’Djamena. It is popular and has been dubbed the Babel Tower of the modern world.

It is home to more than 200 ethnic groups and houses more than 100 languages. However, the majority of their people are either Arabic or French speaking.

17. Greenland – Europe

Another geographical irony arises in the case of Greenland. Unlike Iceland, 80% of Greenland is covered with ice sheets. It is the largest island but has a low population of about 56,000.

Greenland is part of Denmark, and it’s close to Canada with a proximity of 26km. The most interesting fact about Greenland is that it holds about 7% of the world’s reserve of fresh water.

18. Tajikistan - Asia

Tajikistan stands out most when it comes to hiking destinations. It has rugged snow-peaked mountainous regions that are popular among tourists.

The name can trouble you pronouncing, especially for the first time or if you are not a native of the Asian region. But, together with the rest of the ‘stan’ countries, Tajikistan has one of the funny country names in Asia.

19. Bangladesh - Asia

Bangladesh is a combination of two names: Bengali, describing the people of the Bengali tribe and Deshi, which means land. Put together, the term Bangladesh means the land of Bengali people.

Again, with modern slang in play, Bangladesh is a funny term if broken down into two words.

20. Burkina Faso - Africa

The list won’t be complete without Burkina Faso. This name sounds a bit funny to most people if you know nothing about Africa. To close the deal, the capital of Burkina Faso is Ouagadougou, which sounds funnier.
