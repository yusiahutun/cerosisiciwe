Kisumu Woman Wins KSh 3.5m SportPesa Mega Jackpot Bonus With Her First-Ever Bet

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 4, 2024
  • Rosemary Auma Opiyo joined SportPesa in December 2023 and only placed her first bet on February 2, 2024
  • As luck would have it, the first stab at the game would be the life-changer, as she predicted 15 out of 17 games correctly
  • The mother of one slept as a modest shop owner and woke up the following morning over KSh 3 million richer

Kisumu resident Rosemary Auma Opiyo is the latest SportPesa Mega Jackpot bonus winner after clinching a staggering KSh 3.5 million.

Auma's remarkable achievement came after she accurately predicted the outcomes of 15 games out of the 17 offered on the betting platform.

Auma won 2 months after joining SportPesa

According to SportPesa Care, the dynamic businesswoman who runs a local general shop joined SportPesa in December 2023.
