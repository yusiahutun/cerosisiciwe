See Zo Kravitz's Alexander Wang Wedding Dress

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 12, 2024
  • Zoë Kravitz shared new images from her wedding to husband Karl Glusman on Instagram earlier this week.
  • The nuptials took place June 29th, 2019 at her father Lenny Kravitz's Paris home.
  • She wore an elegant Alexander Wang gown for the occasion.

Zoë Kravitz finally shared never-before-seen images from her Parisian wedding to Karl Glusman earlier this summer, including a closer look at her stunning wedding gown.

The Big Little Lies star opted for custom Alexander Wang midi ballgown with an embellished portrait neckline and a drop waist. Kravitz completed the angelic look with white kitten heels and a matching petite bow headband, with her braids twisted into a low, tight chignon.

Both Kravitz's stylist, Andrew Mukamal, and Wang himself shared photos of the bride in her elegant wedding day attire. Kravitz has been longtime friends with the designer and has starred in in several of his campaigns. Wang even penned a special note to the actress on Instagram: "FAIRY TALES DO COME TRUE...AND I WAS LUCKY ENOUGH TO WITNESS IT 6-29-2019."

Kravitz shared over a dozen photos from the special day, including sneak peeks at the star-studded guest list (we spotted Donald Glover and Alicia Keys), a moment of Zoë with family before walking down the aisle, and of course plenty of shots of her stunning wedding gown.

The nuptials took place June 29th, 2019 at her father, Lenny Kravitz’s stunning three-story, eight-bedroom, 18th century home in Paris, France. Alongside her father, Zoë was joined by her mother Lisa Bonet, her husband Jason Momoa, and their two children (and Zoë’s younger half siblings), Lola Iolani and Nakoa-Wolf Manakauapo Namakaeha.

Among the multiple gorgeous, black and white images, Zoë also made sure to post an endearing moment with her Big Little Lies costars, who were also in attendance. In the photo, Reese Witherspoon, Laura Dern, Nicole Kidman, and Shailene Woodley all gather around Kravitz for a sweet reunion during the reception.

Along with the dozens of other comments from celebrity A-listers who expressed their congrats to Kravitz and Glusman on their nuptials, Laura Dern made sure to send an extra sweet note to her co-star.

“[It was] the most special day,” the actress commented.
