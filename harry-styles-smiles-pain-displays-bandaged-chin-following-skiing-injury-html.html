He is believed to have injured himself on his recent skiing holiday with Taylor Swift.

But Harry Styles attempted to smile through the pain on Christmas Eve as he displayed the extent of his injuries in a picture posted on Twitter.

The One Direction singer was seen with a bandage on his chin as he did his best to put on a brave front at what appeared to be a Christmas celebration.

Ouch! Harry Styles displayed a bandaged chin in this picture posted on Twitter following his skiing injury

Harry has remained silent about the nature of his injury on Twitter, only posting the cryptic: 'Chin chinnigan, chin chinnigan...' in reference to it.

And it is believed that the injury occurred during Harry's time at the ski resort with girlfriend Taylor Swift last week.

Arriving back at London's Heathrow Airport on Monday, the 18-year-old did his best to keep his chin covered by wearing a black roll-neck and high-collared coat.

Covering up: Harry hid his injury while wearing a black roll-neck and high-collared coat while arriving back at London's Heathrow airport on Monday

Hiding away: Harry kept his head down so no-one could see his skiing injury

Harry and Taylor had decided to enjoy a minibreak together ahead of spending Christmas apart.

While Taylor is heading out to Australia to promote her new album and perform various gigs, Harry is remaining in the UK with family.

A source said: 'Harry and Taylor have spent a lot of time together recently as they really enjoy each other’s company, which is why they decided to go on the skiing mini-break together this weekend.

'They’re both massive stars and Taylor already had the commitment to go to Australia and Harry’s family were really keen to see him over the holidays. But they’re already planning their next meet.

'Taylor has suggested Harry flies to Australia to meet her.'
